## 📚 New Vocabulary

---

### 1. 旅游 — lǚ yóu — _to travel, to go on a trip (v.)_

> Common pattern: **去 qù + place + 旅游** = to go travel / sightsee in…

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你喜欢旅游吗？|Nǐ xǐhuan lǚyóu ma?|Do you like travelling?|
|你喜欢去哪儿旅游？|Nǐ xǐhuan qù nǎr lǚyóu?|Where do you like to travel?|
|这个月你想去旅游吗？|Zhège yuè nǐ xiǎng qù lǚyóu ma?|Do you want to go on a trip this month?|
|去日本旅游贵吗？|Qù Rìběn lǚyóu guì ma?|Is travelling to Japan expensive?|

---

### 2. 觉得 — jué de — _to think, to find, to feel (v.)_

> Used to express a personal opinion or a subjective impression.

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你觉得今天冷吗？|Nǐ juéde jīntiān lěng ma?|Do you think it's cold today?|
|你觉得饺子好吃吗？|Nǐ juéde jiǎozi hǎochī ma?|Do you think dumplings are tasty?|
|你觉得去日本旅游贵吗？|Nǐ juéde qù Rìběn lǚyóu guì ma?|Do you think travelling to Japan is expensive?|
|明天我们10:00见，你觉得怎么样？|Míngtiān wǒmen shí diǎn jiàn, nǐ juéde zěnmeyàng?|We'll meet at 10 tomorrow, what do you think?|

---

### 3. 最 — zuì — _the most, to the highest degree (adv.)_

> **Structure:** 最 + adjective **or** 最 + psychological verb (喜欢, 想…)

|Expression|Pinyin|Meaning|
|---|---|---|
|最好|zuì hǎo|the best / best of all|
|最多|zuì duō|the most|
|最少|zuì shǎo|the fewest / least|
|最贵|zuì guì|the most expensive|
|最漂亮|zuì piàoliang|the most beautiful|
|最喜欢|zuì xǐhuan|to like best (favourite)|
|最不喜欢|zuì bù xǐhuan|to like least|
|最想|zuì xiǎng|to want most|

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|大卫的英语最好。|Dàwèi de yīngyǔ zuì hǎo.|David's English is the best.|
|我最喜欢吃米饭。|Wǒ zuì xǐhuan chī mǐfàn.|What I like eating most is rice.|
|它的眼睛最漂亮。|Tā de yǎnjing zuì piàoliang.|Its eyes are the most beautiful.|

---

### 4. 为什么 / 因为 — wèi shénme / yīn wèi — _why / because_

> Question–answer pair: **为什么 ?** → answer with **因为…**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|为什么她今天没来工作？|Wèi shénme tā jīntiān méi lái gōngzuò?|Why didn't she come to work today?|
|因为她今天身体不好。|Yīnwèi tā jīntiān shēntǐ bù hǎo.|Because she isn't feeling well today.|
|为什么他不去外面？|Wèi shénme tā bú qù wàimiàn?|Why isn't he going outside?|
|因为下雨了。|Yīnwèi xià yǔ le.|Because it's raining.|

---

### 5. 也 — yě — _also, too (adv.)_

> Placed before the verb, never at the end of the sentence.

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|我很喜欢旅游，你也喜欢旅游吗？|Wǒ hěn xǐhuan lǚyóu, nǐ yě xǐhuan lǚyóu ma?|I love travelling, do you like travelling too?|
|现在我觉得很冷，你也觉得冷吗？|Xiànzài wǒ juéde hěn lěng, nǐ yě juéde lěng ma?|Right now I find it cold, do you too?|
|我爱吃中国菜，你也爱吃吗？|Wǒ ài chī Zhōngguó cài, nǐ yě ài chī ma?|I love eating Chinese food, do you too?|

---

### 6. 运动 — yùn dòng — _sport; to exercise (v./n.)_

> Used as a **noun** (做运动 / 什么运动) or as a **verb** (今天我要运动).

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你最喜欢做什么运动？|Nǐ zuì xǐhuan zuò shénme yùndòng?|Which sport do you like best?|
|你喜欢早上还是晚上运动？|Nǐ xǐhuan zǎoshang háishi wǎnshang yùndòng?|Do you prefer to exercise in the morning or the evening?|
|你一个星期运动多少个小时？|Nǐ yí ge xīngqī yùndòng duōshǎo ge xiǎoshí?|How many hours a week do you exercise?|
|运动前，你做什么？运动后呢？|Yùndòng qián, nǐ zuò shénme? Yùndòng hòu ne?|What do you do before exercising? And after?|

### 7. 踢足球 — tī zúqiú — _to play football_

> 踢 tī = to kick · 足球 zúqiú = football (soccer)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|Collins 先生最喜欢的运动是踢足球。|Collins xiānsheng zuì xǐhuan de yùndòng shì tī zúqiú.|Mr Collins's favourite sport is football.|
|每个星期六下午他都和朋友去踢足球。|Měi ge xīngqī liù xiàwǔ tā dōu hé péngyou qù tī zúqiú.|Every Saturday afternoon he plays football with friends.|
|晚上和朋友去酒吧喝啤酒和在电视上看足球。|Wǎnshang hé péngyou qù jiǔbā hē píjiǔ hé zài diànshì shàng kàn zúqiú.|In the evening he goes to the bar with friends to drink beer and watch football on TV.|

### 8. 一起 — yì qǐ — _together, with (adv.)_

> **Structure:** 我/你/他 + 和 + person + **一起** + verb

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你现在和谁在一起？|Nǐ xiànzài hé shuí zài yìqǐ?|Who are you with right now?|
|你喜欢和谁一起去旅游？|Nǐ xǐhuan hé shuí yìqǐ qù lǚyóu?|Who do you like to travel with?|
|你和你的家人一起住在中国吗？|Nǐ hé nǐ de jiārén yìqǐ zhù zài Zhōngguó ma?|Do you live in China with your family?|
|我和朋友一起去商店买东西。|Wǒ hé péngyou yìqǐ qù shāngdiàn mǎi dōngxi.|I go shopping with friends.|

### 9. 要 — yào — _to want, to intend to (aux.)_

> **Structure:** 要 + noun **or** 要 + verb
>
> ⚠️ Negative form: **不想 bù xiǎng** (not 不要)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|我要咖啡。|Wǒ yào kāfēi.|I want a coffee.|
|你要吃米饭吗？|Nǐ yào chī mǐfàn ma?|Do you want to eat rice?|
|我不想吃米饭。|Wǒ bù xiǎng chī mǐfàn.|I don't want to eat rice.|
|今天晚上你要和朋友一起去饭店吃饭吗？|Jīntiān wǎnshang nǐ yào hé péngyou yìqǐ qù fàndiàn chī fàn ma?|Tonight, do you want to go eat at a restaurant with friends?|

## 🔤 Grammar

---

### 1. The auxiliary verb 要 yào

#### _"To want to do something / to intend to…"_

> Placed before a verb, 要 expresses will or intention. Its negative form is **不想 bù xiǎng** (not 不要, which means "must not do").
>
> **Structure:** Subject + **要** + Verb/Noun

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|王方要学习英语。|Wáng Fāng yào xuéxí yīngyǔ.|Wang Fang wants to learn English.|
|我们要不要买几个新的椅子？|Wǒmen yào bú yào mǎi jǐ ge xīn de yǐzi?|Shall we buy a few new chairs or not?|
|小王要去，我不想去。|Xiǎo Wáng yào qù, wǒ bù xiǎng qù.|Xiao Wang wants to go, I don't want to.|

---

### 2. The degree adverb 最 zuì

#### _"The most… / The best…"_

> **最** expresses the superlative. It goes directly before an adjective or a psychological verb (喜欢, 想, 爱…).
>
> **Structure:** 最 + Adjective **or** 最 + Psychological verb

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|大卫的英语最好。|Dàwèi de yīngyǔ zuì hǎo.|David's English is the best.|
|我最喜欢吃米饭。|Wǒ zuì xǐhuan chī mǐfàn.|What I like eating most is rice.|
|它的眼睛最漂亮。|Tā de yǎnjing zuì piàoliang.|Its eyes are the most beautiful.|

---

### 3. Approximate quantity expressions — 几 jǐ and 多 duō

#### _"A few / About ten…"_

> **几 jǐ** indicates an indefinite number below 10 ("a few"), followed by a measure word.
>
> **Structure:** 几 + Measure word + Noun

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|车上有几个人。|Chē shàng yǒu jǐ ge rén.|There are a few people in the car.|
|我想买几本书。|Wǒ xiǎng mǎi jǐ běn shū.|I want to buy a few books.|
|我们要不要买几个新的椅子？|Wǒmen yào bú yào mǎi jǐ ge xīn de yǐzi?|Shall we buy a few new chairs?|

> **Combinations with 十 shí:**
>
> - 十几个人 shí jǐ ge rén → about ten people (between 11 and 19)
> - 几十个人 jǐ shí ge rén → dozens of people (between 20 and 90)

---

> **多 duō** expresses a value slightly above a given number ("-something / and a bit").
>
> ⚠️ If the number is **< 10**: 多 goes **after** the measure word. If the number is **≥ 10** (a round number): 多 goes **before** the measure word.

|Structure|Example|Meaning|
|---|---|---|
|Num. + M.W. + **多** + Noun (< 10)|三个多星期 sān ge duō xīngqī|more than 3 weeks|
|Num. + M.W. + **多** + Noun (< 10)|六个多月 liù ge duō yuè|more than 6 months|
|Num. + **多** + M.W. + Noun (≥ 10)|十多个月 shí duō ge yuè|about ten months (10 to 19)|
|Num. + **多** + M.W. + Noun (≥ 10)|二十多块钱 èrshí duō kuài qián|twenty-something yuan|

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|今天我和几个朋友喝酒。|Jīntiān wǒ hé jǐ ge péngyou hē jiǔ.|Today I had a drink with a few friends.|
|这本书二十多块。|Zhè běn shū èrshí duō kuài.|This book costs twenty-something yuan.|
|我的猫三岁多了。|Wǒ de māo sān suì duō le.|My cat is a little over 3 years old.|

## 💬 Dialogues

---

### Dialogue 1 — 在学校 zài xuéxiào (_At school_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|我要去北京旅游，你觉得什么时候去最好？|Wǒ yào qù Běijīng lǚyóu, nǐ juéde shénme shíhou qù zuì hǎo?|I want to travel to Beijing, when do you think is the best time to go?|
|B|九月去北京旅游最好。|Jiǔ yuè qù Běijīng lǚyóu zuì hǎo.|September is the best time to travel to Beijing.|
|A|为什么？|Wèi shénme?|Why?|
|B|九月的北京天气不冷也不热。|Jiǔ yuè de Běijīng tiānqì bù lěng yě bú rè.|In September the weather in Beijing is neither cold nor hot.|

**Question:** 什么时候去北京旅游最好？为什么？ _(When is the best time to travel to Beijing? Why?)_

### Dialogue 2 — 看照片 kàn zhàopiàn (_Looking at a photo_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|你喜欢什么运动？|Nǐ xǐhuan shénme yùndòng?|Which sport do you like?|
|B|我最喜欢踢足球。|Wǒ zuì xǐhuan tī zúqiú.|My favourite is football.|
|A|下午我们一起去踢足球吧。|Xiàwǔ wǒmen yìqǐ qù tī zúqiú ba.|Let's go play football together this afternoon!|
|B|好啊！|Hǎo a!|Great!|

**Question:** 他们下午要做什么？ _(What are they going to do this afternoon?)_

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|

## 🔡 Phonetics

> **Stress in two-syllable words**
>
> **(1) "Medium + Stressed" pattern**: most words — the 2nd syllable is stressed and longer. Examples: 冰箱 bīngxiāng · 旅游 lǚyóu · 高兴 gāoxìng · 开始 kāishǐ
>
> **(2) "Stressed + Neutral" pattern**: a minority of words — the 1st syllable is strong, the 2nd is short and toneless. Examples: 东西 dōngxi · 枕头 zhěntou · 你们 nǐmen · 故事 gùshi

## ✍️ Characters

> **Strokes (7): 乙 and 乛**
>
> - 乙 héngzhétí (horizontal-turning-rising) → e.g. 话 huà, 说 shuō
> - 乛 héngzhézhégōu (horizontal-triple-turning-hook) → e.g. 奶 nǎi, 场 chǎng

> **Standalone characters:**
>
> - 为 wéi: the complex form 爲 looks like a hand leading an elephant to work → original meaning: "to work"
> - 也 yě: used to look like a snake with a pointed head → over time the original meaning was lost and it became an adverb

> **Radicals:**
>
> - 王字旁 (王): related to jade → e.g. 现 xiàn (now), 球 qiú (ball)
> - 足字旁 (足): related to feet → e.g. 跑 pǎo (to run), 踢 tī (to kick)

|Key point|Structure|Example|
|---|---|---|
|Travel somewhere|去 + place + 旅游|去北京旅游 qù Běijīng lǚyóu|
