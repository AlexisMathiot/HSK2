
# zuǒbiān nà ge hóngsè de shì wǒ de

_"The red one on the left is mine."_

---

## 📚 New Vocabulary

---

### 1. 手表 — shǒu biǎo — _watch (n.)_

> 手 shǒu = hand · 表 biǎo = watch
> 
> Measure word: **块 kuài** → 一块手表 yí kuài shǒubiǎo = one watch

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|这块手表多少钱？|Zhè kuài shǒubiǎo duōshǎo qián?|How much is this watch?|
|他的手表好看吗？|Tā de shǒubiǎo hǎokàn ma?|Is his watch nice-looking?|
|看看他的手表，现在几点了？|Kàn kan tā de shǒubiǎo, xiànzài jǐ diǎn le?|Look at his watch, what time is it?|

### 2. 千 — qiān — _thousand (num.)_

> Recap of large numbers:
> 
> - 百 bǎi = hundred
> - 千 qiān = thousand
> - 万 wàn = ten thousand (10,000)
> - 亿 yì = hundred million

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|这块手表三千多块。|Zhè kuài shǒubiǎo sān qiān duō kuài.|This watch costs over 3,000 yuan.|
|中国有多少人吗？|Zhōngguó yǒu duōshǎo rén ma?|How many people are there in China?|
|你的国家有多少人？|Nǐ de guójiā yǒu duōshǎo rén?|How many people are there in your country?|

### 5. 一下 — yí xià — _verbal particle (indicates a brief action)_

> **Verb + 一下** = to do something briefly / to give something a try
> 
> Equivalent to: **Verb + Verb** (e.g. 看看) or **Verb + 一 + Verb** (e.g. 看一看)

|Structure|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|看一下|我看一下。|Wǒ kàn yíxià.|I'll take a quick look.|
|休息一下|你休息一下吧。|Nǐ xiūxi yíxià ba.|Have a little rest.|
|问一下|我问一下老师。|Wǒ wèn yíxià lǎoshī.|I'll go ask the teacher.|
|试一下|你试一下吧！|Nǐ shì yíxià ba!|Give it a try!|

## 🔤 Grammar

---

### 1. The nominal "的 de" structure

#### _Replacing a noun with "的" after an adjective, verb or pronoun_

> When the context is clear, the noun can be omitted and **的 de** used alone. It acts as a noun phrase (= "the one that…").
> 
> **Structure:** Pronoun / Adjective / Verb + **的** (= the one…)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|这本书不是我的。（=我的书）|Zhè běn shū bú shì wǒ de.|This book isn't mine.|
|这个杯子是昨天买的。（=昨天买的杯子）|Zhè ge bēizi shì zuótiān mǎi de.|This cup is the one (I) bought yesterday.|
|这块手表是你的吗？（=你的手表）|Zhè kuài shǒubiǎo shì nǐ de ma?|Is this watch yours?|
|不是我的，是我丈夫的。|Bú shì wǒ de, shì wǒ zhàngfu de.|It's not mine, it's my husband's.|

> **Practice questions:**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|她喜欢红色的还是粉色的？|Tā xǐhuan hóngsè de háishì fěnsè de?|Does she prefer the red one or the pink one?|
|你想要大杯的还是小杯的？|Nǐ xiǎng yào dà bēi de háishì xiǎo bēi de?|Do you want the large one or the small one?|
|这个手机是苹果的还是小米的？|Zhè ge shǒujī shì Píngguǒ de háishì XiǎoMǐ de?|Is this phone an iPhone or a Xiaomi?|
|这个电脑是2020年买的吗？|Zhè ge diànnǎo shì 2020 nián mǎi de ma?|Was this computer bought in 2020?|

---

### 2. Verb + 一下 — yí xià

#### _Expressing a brief action or an attempt_

> **一下 yí xià** placed after a verb expresses a short or light action. Same meaning as repeating the verb (AA) or the **Verb + 一 + Verb** structure.

|Structure|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|我 + V + 一下|我看一下。|Wǒ kàn yíxià.|I'll take a quick look.|
|你 + V + 一下 + 吧|你休息一下吧。|Nǐ xiūxi yíxià ba.|Have a little rest.|
|我 + V + 一下 + Object|我问一下老师。|Wǒ wèn yíxià lǎoshī.|I'll go ask the teacher.|

> ⚠️ These three forms are equivalent:
> 
> - 看一下 kàn yíxià = 看看 kàn kan = 看一看 kàn yi kàn

---

### 3. 真 + adjective — zhēn + adj.

#### _Expressing exclamation / admiration_

> **真 zhēn** before an adjective intensifies the expression and gives an exclamatory tone. Equivalent to "really!", "how … !", "so … !"
> 
> **Structure:** Subject + **真** + Adjective + ！

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你真好！|Nǐ zhēn hǎo!|You're really kind!|
|今天天气真好！|Jīntiān tiānqì zhēn hǎo!|The weather is really nice!|
|你女儿的房间真漂亮！|Nǐ nǚér de fángjiān zhēn piàoliang!|Your daughter's room is really beautiful!|
|这里真漂亮！|Zhèlǐ zhēn piàoliang!|It's really beautiful here!|
|今天真累啊！|Jīntiān zhēn lèi a!|I'm really tired today!|

## 💬 Dialogues

---

### Dialogue 1 — 在房间 zài fángjiān (_In the room_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|这块手表是你的吗？|Zhè kuài shǒubiǎo shì nǐ de ma?|Is this watch yours?|
|B|不是我的。是我爸爸的。|Bú shì wǒ de. Shì wǒ bàba de.|It's not mine. It's my dad's.|
|A|多少钱买的？|Duōshǎo qián mǎi de?|How much did it cost?|
|B|三千多块。|Sānqiān duō kuài.|Over 3,000 yuan.|

**Question:** 爸爸的手表多少钱？Bàba de shǒubiǎo duōshǎo qián?

---

### Dialogue 2 — 在家里 zài jiālǐ (_At home_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|这是今天早上的报纸吗？|Zhè shì jīntiān zǎoshang de bàozhǐ ma?|Is this this morning's newspaper?|
|B|不是，是昨天的。|Bú shì, shì zuótiān de.|No, it's yesterday's.|
|A|你听，是不是送报纸的来了？|Nǐ tīng, shì bu shì sòng bàozhǐ de lái le?|Listen, is that the newspaper delivery person who's come?|
|B|我看一下。不是，是送牛奶的。|Wǒ kàn yíxià. Bú shì, shì sòng niúnǎi de.|I'll take a look. No, it's the milk delivery person.|

**Question:** 送报纸的来了吗？Sòng bàozhǐ de lái le ma?

---

### Dialogue 3 — 在家里 zài jiālǐ (_At home — the pink room_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|这是谁的房间？|Zhè shì shéi de fángjiān?|Whose room is this?|
|B|这是我和我丈夫的，旁边那个小的房间是我女儿的。|Zhè shì wǒ hé wǒ zhàngfu de, pángbiān nà ge xiǎo de fángjiān shì wǒ nǚér de.|This is my husband's and mine. The small one next to it is my daughter's.|
|A|你女儿的房间真漂亮！都是粉色的。|Nǐ nǚér de fángjiān zhēn piàoliang! Dōu shì fěnsè de.|Your daughter's room is really beautiful! It's all pink.|
|B|是啊，粉色是我女儿最喜欢的颜色。|Shì a, fěnsè shì wǒ nǚér zuì xǐhuan de yánsè.|Yes, pink is my daughter's favourite colour.|

**Questions:** 旁边那个小的房间是谁的？她女儿的房间怎么样？

---

### Dialogue 4 — 在办公室 zài bàngōngshì (_At the office_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|你看见我的杯子了吗？|Nǐ kànjiàn wǒ de bēizi le ma?|Have you seen my cup?|
|B|这里有几个杯子，哪个是你的？|Zhèlǐ yǒu jǐ ge bēizi, nǎge shì nǐ de?|There are several cups here, which one is yours?|
|A|左边那个红色的是我的。|Zuǒbiān nà ge hóngsè de shì wǒ de.|The red one on the left is mine.|

**Question:** 哪个杯子是她的？Nǎge bēizi shì tā de?

## 💡 Quick Recap

|Key point|Structure|Example|
|---|---|---|
|"The one that…"|Adj. / V / Pronoun + 的|我的 wǒ de / 昨天买的 zuótiān mǎi de|
|Brief action|Verb + 一下 yíxià|看一下 kàn yíxià / 试一下 shì yíxià|
|Exclamation|真 zhēn + adj.|真漂亮！zhēn piàoliang!|
|Position|A + 在 zài + B + position word|图书馆在教学楼后边|
|Colour + noun|颜色 yánsè + 的 + noun|红色的杯子 hóngsè de bēizi|
