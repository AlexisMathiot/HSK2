
# zhè ge gōngzuò shì tā bāng wǒ jièshào de

_"He's the one who recommended me for this job."_

---

## 📚 New Vocabulary

---

### 1. 生日 — shēng rì — _birthday (n.)_

> 生 shēng = to give birth · 日 rì = day

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你的生日是几月几号？|Nǐ de shēngrì shì jǐ yuè jǐ hào?|When is your birthday?|
|祝你生日快乐！|Zhù nǐ shēngrì kuàilè!|Happy birthday!|
|去年生日你做了什么？|Qùnián shēngrì nǐ zuò le shénme?|What did you do for your birthday last year?|

---

### 2. 快乐 — kuài lè — _happy, joyful (adj.)_

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|生日快乐！|Shēngrì kuàilè!|Happy birthday!|
|新年快乐！|Xīnnián kuàilè!|Happy New Year!|
|祝你新年快乐！|Zhù nǐ xīnnián kuàilè!|I wish you a happy New Year!|

### 3. 给 — gěi — _to give / to, for (prep.)_

> ⚠️ Two equivalent constructions:
> 
> **Person 1 + Verb + Object + 给 (gěi) + Person 2**
> 
> **Person 1 + 给 (gěi) + Person 2 + Verb + Object**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|Tom 给了 Rosa 一个礼物。|Tom gěi le Rosa yí ge lǐwù.|Tom gave Rosa a present.|
|我给你买了衣服。|Wǒ gěi nǐ mǎi le yīfu.|I bought you some clothes.|
|她给朋友打电话。|Tā gěi péngyou dǎ diànhuà.|She calls her friend.|
|爸爸给我们做饭。|Bàba gěi wǒmen zuò fàn.|Dad cooks for us.|

---

### 4. 接 — jiē — _to receive, to pick up (the phone) (v.)_

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|她在接电话呢。|Tā zài jiē diànhuà ne.|She's answering the phone.|
|你可以接一下电话吗？|Nǐ kěyǐ jiē yíxià diànhuà ma?|Can you answer the phone?|
|今天是谁接电话的？|Jīntiān shì shéi jiē diànhuà de?|Who answered the phone today?|

---

### 5. 晚上 — wǎn shang — _evening, night (n.)_

> Progression of the day: 早上 zǎoshang → 上午 shàngwǔ → 中午 zhōngwǔ → 下午 xiàwǔ → **晚上 wǎnshang** _(early morning → late morning → noon → afternoon → evening)_

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|今天晚上你想去哪儿？|Jīntiān wǎnshang nǐ xiǎng qù nǎr?|Where do you want to go tonight?|
|昨天晚上你和谁一起吃饭？|Zuótiān wǎnshang nǐ hé shéi yìqǐ chī fàn?|Who did you have dinner with last night?|
|明天晚上你有时间吗？|Míngtiān wǎnshang nǐ yǒu shíjiān ma?|Are you free tomorrow evening?|

---

### 6. 问 — wèn — _to ask, to ask a question (v.)_

> Useful expressions:
> 
> - 请问 qǐng wèn = may I ask… / excuse me
> - 问题 wèntí = question / problem
> - 没问题 méi wèntí = no problem

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|请问，你的生日是几月几号？|Qǐng wèn, nǐ de shēngrì shì jǐ yuè jǐ hào?|Excuse me, when is your birthday?|
|你喜欢问问题吗？|Nǐ xǐhuan wèn wèntí ma?|Do you like asking questions?|
|晚上我问一下儿子。|Wǎnshang wǒ wèn yíxià érzi.|I'll ask my son this evening.|

### 7. 非常 — fēi cháng — _very, extremely (adv.)_

> Used as: **非常 + adjective** or **非常 + psychological verb**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|今天的天气非常好。|Jīntiān de tiānqì fēicháng hǎo.|The weather is very nice today.|
|我非常喜欢吃苹果。|Wǒ fēicháng xǐhuan chī píngguǒ.|I love eating apples.|
|我非常想去巴黎旅游。|Wǒ fēicháng xiǎng qù Bālí lǚyóu.|I really want to travel to Paris.|

---

### 8. 开始 — kāi shǐ — _to begin, to start (v.)_

> Antonym: 结束 jiéshù = to finish, to end

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|电影几点开始？|Diànyǐng jǐ diǎn kāishǐ?|What time does the film start?|
|今天中文课几点开始？|Jīntiān zhōngwén kè jǐ diǎn kāishǐ?|What time does the Chinese class start today?|

---

### 9. 已经 — yǐ jīng — _already (adv.)_

> Usually used with **了 le** at the end of the sentence.

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|现在已经12:00了吗？|Xiànzài yǐjīng 12:00 le ma?|Is it already noon?|
|你已经吃午饭了吗？|Nǐ yǐjīng chī wǔfàn le ma?|Have you already had lunch?|
|你已经学中文多少个月了？|Nǐ yǐjīng xué zhōngwén duōshǎo ge yuè le?|How many months have you been learning Chinese now?|

---

### 10. 长 — cháng — _long (adj.)_

> Antonym: 短 duǎn = short
> 
> Useful questions:
> 
> - 多长？duō cháng = how long (in length)?
> - 多长时间？duō cháng shíjiān = how long (in time)?

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|她的头发是长的还是短的？|Tā de tóufa shì cháng de háishì duǎn de?|Is her hair long or short?|
|这个电影是多长时间的？|Zhè ge diànyǐng shì duō cháng shíjiān de?|How long is this film?|
|你学中文多长时间了？|Nǐ xué zhōngwén duō cháng shíjiān le?|How long have you been learning Chinese?|

### 11. 两 — liǎng — _two (num.)_

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|

|两年|liǎng nián|two years|

## 🔤 Grammar

---

### 1. The 是……的 structure — shì……de

#### _Highlighting the agent of a past action_

> When an action has already happened, **是……的** is used to emphasise **who** did it, **when**, or **how**.
> 
> **Structure:** Object + **是** + Who + Verb + **的**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|这本书是我买的。|Zhè běn shū shì wǒ mǎi de.|I'm the one who bought this book.|
|晚饭是妈妈做的。|Wǎnfàn shì māma zuò de.|Mum is the one who made dinner.|
|电话是谁打的？|Diànhuà shì shéi dǎ de?|Who made this phone call?|
|这个工作是他帮我介绍的。|Zhège gōngzuò shì tā bāng wǒ jièshào de.|He's the one who recommended me for this job.|

> **Negative** → add **不 bù** before 是:

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|电话不是我接的。|Diànhuà bú shì wǒ jiē de.|I'm not the one who answered the phone.|
|苹果不是王方买的。|Píngguǒ bú shì Wáng Fāng mǎi de.|Wang Fang isn't the one who bought the apples.|

---

### 2. ……的时候 — de shíhòu

#### _"when…", "at the moment when…"_

> Two uses:
> 
> - **Number/age + 的时候** → at such a moment / at such an age
> - **Verb + 的时候** → while / when doing something

**With an age or a moment:**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|我十一岁的时候开始踢足球。|Wǒ shíyī suì de shíhòu kāishǐ tī zúqiú.|When I was eleven, I started playing football.|
|今天早上八点的时候我没在家。|Jīntiān zǎoshang bā diǎn de shíhòu wǒ méi zài jiā.|At eight o'clock this morning I wasn't home.|

**With a verb:**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|我睡觉的时候，妈妈在做饭。|Wǒ shuìjiào de shíhòu, māma zài zuò fàn.|While I was sleeping, Mum was cooking.|
|在巴黎旅游的时候，他们都很高兴。|Zài Bālí lǚyóu de shíhòu, tāmen dōu hěn gāoxìng.|While travelling in Paris, they were all very happy.|
|你去商店的时候，可以帮我买个东西吗？|Nǐ qù shāngdiàn de shíhòu, kěyǐ bāng wǒ mǎi ge dōngxi ma?|When you go to the shop, can you buy me something?|

---

### 3. 已经……了 — yǐjīng……le

#### _"already"_

> Indicates that an action is **completed** or that a situation has reached a certain degree.
> 
> **Structure:** Subject + **已经** + Verb/Adj + **了**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|王老师已经回家了。|Wáng lǎoshī yǐjīng huí jiā le.|Teacher Wang has already gone home.|
|我的身体已经好了。|Wǒ de shēntǐ yǐjīng hǎo le.|I've already recovered.|
|我已经学了第三课了。|Wǒ yǐjīng xué le dì sān kè le.|I've already studied lesson 3.|
|足球我已经踢了十年了。|Zúqiú wǒ yǐjīng tī le shí nián le.|I've been playing football for ten years now.|
|已经两年多了。|Yǐjīng liǎng nián duō le.|It's already been more than two years.|

---

## 💡 Quick Recap

|Key point|Structure|Example|
|---|---|---|
|Who did what|是 + who + V + 的|是谁买的？shì shéi mǎi de?|
|When / while|……的时候 de shíhòu|我十八岁的时候 wǒ shíbā suì de shíhòu|
|Already|已经……了 yǐjīng……le|已经两年了 yǐjīng liǎng nián le|
|Two + measure word|两 liǎng + M|两杯 liǎng bēi / 两年 liǎng nián|
|Give to someone|给 gěi|给你买了 gěi nǐ mǎi le|
