
# jiù mǎi zhè jiàn ba

_"Just get this one."_

---

## 📚 New Vocabulary

---

### 1. 外面 — wài miàn — _outside (n.)_

> Antonym: 里面 lǐmiàn = inside

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|她在咖啡店里面还是外面？|Tā zài kāfēi diàn lǐmiàn háishì wàimiàn?|Is she inside or outside the café?|
|饭店外面有什么？|Fàndiàn wàimiàn yǒu shénme?|What's outside the restaurant?|
|现在你在家还是在外面？|Xiànzài nǐ zài jiā háishì zài wàimiàn?|Are you at home or out right now?|
|今天晚上你想在家吃饭还是去外面吃？|Jīntiān wǎnshang nǐ xiǎng zài jiā chī fàn háishì qù wàimiàn chī?|Tonight do you want to eat at home or eat out?|

---

### 2. 准备 — zhǔn bèi — _to prepare, to plan to (v./n.)_

> Useful expression: 准备好 zhǔnbèi hǎo = to be ready, to have finished preparing

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|他们在准备晚饭。|Tāmen zài zhǔnbèi wǎnfàn.|They're preparing dinner.|
|明年我准备去澳大利亚旅游。|Míngnián wǒ zhǔnbèi qù Àodàlìyà lǚyóu.|Next year I plan to travel to Australia.|
|早饭妈妈给孩子准备了什么？|Zǎofàn māma gěi háizi zhǔnbèi le shénme?|What did Mum prepare for the children's breakfast?|
|去旅游前，你准备什么东西？|Qù lǚyóu qián, nǐ zhǔnbèi shénme dōngxi?|What do you get ready before going on a trip?|

---

### 3. 就 — jiù — _then, in that case (adv.)_

> Indicates a conclusion or resolution drawn from what precedes.

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|今天晚上我们吃什么？就吃匹萨吧。|Jīntiān wǎnshang wǒmen chī shénme? Jiù chī pǐsà ba.|What shall we eat tonight? Let's just have pizza then.|
|外面太热了，我们就在家看书吧。|Wàimiàn tài rè le, wǒmen jiù zài jiā kàn shū ba.|It's too hot outside, let's just stay home and read.|
|你喜欢吃鱼，我们就吃鱼吧。|Nǐ xǐhuan chī yú, wǒmen jiù chī yú ba.|You like fish, so let's have fish.|
|你现在身体不好，就不要喝酒了。|Nǐ xiànzài shēntǐ bù hǎo, jiù búyào hē jiǔ le.|You're not feeling well, so don't drink alcohol.|

---

### 4. 鱼 — yú — _fish (n.)_

> Measure word: 条 tiáo → 一条鱼 yì tiáo yú = one fish

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|这条鱼是什么颜色的？|Zhè tiáo yú shì shénme yánsè de?|What colour is this fish?|
|这条鱼好看吗？|Zhè tiáo yú hǎokàn ma?|Is this fish nice-looking?|
|今天你想吃鱼吗？|Jīntiān nǐ xiǎng chī yú ma?|Do you want to eat fish today?|

---

### 5. 吧 — ba — _suggestion/request particle (part.)_

> Placed at the end of a sentence to indicate a suggestion, a consultation, or a gentle request.

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|今天外面太热了，我们就在家吃饭吧。|Jīntiān wàimiàn tài rè le, wǒmen jiù zài jiā chī fàn ba.|It's too hot outside, let's just eat at home.|
|我们明天晚上8:00见吧！|Wǒmen míngtiān wǎnshang 8:00 jiàn ba!|Let's meet tomorrow at 8 pm!|
|今天是你的生日，我们去外面吃饭吧！|Jīntiān shì nǐ de shēngrì, wǒmen qù wàimiàn chī fàn ba!|It's your birthday, let's go eat out!|

### 6. 件 — jiàn — _measure word for clothes (m.w.)_

|那件衣服|nà jiàn yīfu|that garment|
|哪件衣服？|nǎ jiàn yīfu?|which garment?|
|每件衣服|měi jiàn yīfu|every garment|

### 7. 还 — hái — _fairly, quite (adv.)_

> Softens a positive adjective: "not bad", "okay". A nuance of modesty or lukewarm assessment.
>
> Combinations: 还好 hái hǎo · 还可以 hái kěyǐ · 还行 hái xíng · 还不错 hái búcuò

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你好吗？— 我还好。|Nǐ hǎo ma? — Wǒ hái hǎo.|How are you? — I'm okay.|
|今天的天气怎么样？— 还不错。|Jīntiān de tiānqì zěnmeyàng? — Hái búcuò.|How's the weather today? — Not bad.|
|这个菜怎么样？— 还行。|Zhè ge cài zěnmeyàng? — Hái xíng.|How's this dish? — It's okay.|
|你的中文好吗？— 我的中文还可以。|Nǐ de zhōngwén hǎo ma? — Wǒ de zhōngwén hái kěyǐ.|Is your Chinese good? — My Chinese is okay.|

### 8. 可以 — kě yǐ — _passable (adj.) / can, may (v.)_

> **Adjective:** passable, not bad (often with 还 hái)
> **Verb:** can, to be allowed

|Example (adj.)|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|爸爸做的菜还可以。|Bàba zuò de cài hái kěyǐ.|The food Dad makes is okay.|
|这个店还可以，不太贵。|Zhè ge diàn hái kěyǐ, bú tài guì.|This shop is okay, not too expensive.|

|Example (v.)|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|我可以说英语。|Wǒ kěyǐ shuō yīngyǔ.|I can speak English.|
|你可以帮我吗？|Nǐ kěyǐ bāng wǒ ma?|Can you help me?|
|今天我们可以去外面吃饭吗？|Jīntiān wǒmen kěyǐ qù wàimiàn chī fàn ma?|Can we go eat out today?|

### 12. 咖啡 — kā fēi — _coffee (n.)_

### 13. 对 — duì — _correct (adj.) / towards, for (prep.)_

> **Adjective:** correct, right → 对不对？duì bu duì? = is that right?
> **Preposition:** towards, for → **A 对 B + adj.**

|Example (adj.)|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你住在中国，对吗？|Nǐ zhù zài Zhōngguó, duì ma?|You live in China, right?|
|你会说中文，对不对？|Nǐ huì shuō zhōngwén, duì bu duì?|You can speak Chinese, can't you?|

|Example (prep.)|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|运动对身体好。|Yùndòng duì shēntǐ hǎo.|Exercise is good for your health.|
|喝太多酒对身体不好。|Hē tài duō jiǔ duì shēntǐ bù hǎo.|Drinking too much alcohol is bad for your health.|
|看太多电视对眼睛不好。|Kàn tài duō diànshì duì yǎnjīng bù hǎo.|Watching too much TV is bad for your eyes.|
|他对我很好。|Tā duì wǒ hěn hǎo.|He's very kind to me.|

---

### 14. 以后 — yǐ hòu — _after, later, in the future (n.)_

> Antonym: 以前 yǐqián = before, previously
>
> Short forms: 后 hòu = after · 前 qián = before
>
> Timeline: **以前 yǐqián** (before) → **现在 xiànzài** (now) → **以后 yǐhòu** (after)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|以前我在广州工作。|Yǐqián wǒ zài Guǎngzhōu gōngzuò.|Before, I worked in Guangzhou.|
|现在在上海工作。|Xiànzài zài Shànghǎi gōngzuò.|Now I work in Shanghai.|
|以后我想去北京工作。|Yǐhòu wǒ xiǎng qù Běijīng gōngzuò.|Later I'd like to work in Beijing.|
|上课以前，你喝了一杯咖啡吗？|Shàngkè yǐqián, nǐ hē le yì bēi kāfēi ma?|Before class, did you have a coffee?|
|下课以后，你想做什么？|Xiàkè yǐhòu, nǐ xiǎng zuò shénme?|After class, what do you want to do?|

## 🔤 Grammar

---

### 1. The adverb 就 jiù

#### _Drawing a conclusion / decision from what precedes_

> **就 + verb** indicates a conclusion or resolution based on what was said before.

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你不想去，就在家休息吧。|Nǐ bù xiǎng qù, jiù zài jiā xiūxi ba.|You don't want to go, so just stay home and rest.|
|这儿的咖啡不错，就喝咖啡吧。|Zhèr de kāfēi búcuò, jiù hē kāfēi ba.|The coffee here is good, so let's have coffee.|
|就做你爱吃的鱼吧。|Jiù zuò nǐ ài chī de yú ba.|Let's just make the fish you like, then.|

> **就是 jiù shì** = indicates a concession ("it's just that…")

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|这个菜很好吃，就是有点儿辣。|Zhè ge cài hěn hǎo chī, jiù shì yǒu diǎnr là.|This dish is delicious, it's just a bit spicy.|
|今天我不错，就是有点累。|Jīntiān wǒ búcuò, jiù shì yǒu diǎn lèi.|I'm fine today, it's just that I'm a bit tired.|

---

### 2. The modal adverb 还 hái

#### _"Passable, acceptable"_

> **还 + adjective** means something is passable, acceptable (neither excellent nor bad).

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你身体怎么样？— 还好。|Nǐ shēntǐ zěnmeyàng? — Hái hǎo.|How's your health? — Okay.|
|这件衣服大吗？— 还行，不太大。|Zhè jiàn yīfu dà ma? — Hái xíng, bù tài dà.|Is this garment big? — It's okay, not too big.|
|昨天的考试怎么样？— 听和说还可以，读和写不好。|Zuótiān de kǎoshì zěnmeyàng? — Tīng hé shuō hái kěyǐ, dú hé xiě bù hǎo.|How was yesterday's exam? — Listening and speaking were okay, reading and writing not so good.|

---

### 3. The adverbial modifier 有点儿 yǒu diǎnr vs 一点儿 yì diǎnr

#### _"A little" — negative connotation vs neutral_

> **有点儿 + adj./psychological verb** → expresses a negative mood or a complaint
>
> **一点儿 + noun** → a small quantity
>
> **adj. + 一点儿** → a little more + adj. (comparison)

**有点儿 yǒu diǎnr (complaint/negative):**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|今天天气有点儿冷。|Jīntiān tiānqì yǒu diǎnr lěng.|The weather is a bit cold today.|
|我昨天有点儿累。|Wǒ zuótiān yǒu diǎnr lèi.|I was a bit tired yesterday.|
|这件衣服有点儿大。|Zhè jiàn yīfu yǒu diǎnr dà.|This garment is a bit big.|
|她有点儿不喜欢住在这里。|Tā yǒu diǎnr bù xǐhuan zhù zài zhèlǐ.|She rather dislikes living here.|

**一点儿 yì diǎnr (quantity / comparison):**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|她会说一点儿法语。|Tā huì shuō yì diǎnr fǎyǔ.|She can speak a little French.|
|杯子里有一点儿水。|Bēizi lǐ yǒu yì diǎnr shuǐ.|There's a little water in the glass.|
|这个杯子有点儿贵，那个杯子便宜一点儿。|Zhè ge bēizi yǒu diǎnr guì, nà ge bēizi piányí yì diǎnr.|This cup is a bit expensive, that one is a little cheaper.|
|她的头发长一点儿，妹妹的头发短一点儿。|Tā de tóufa cháng yì diǎnr, mèimei de tóufa duǎn yì diǎnr.|Her hair is a little longer, her little sister's hair is a little shorter.|

## 💬 Dialogues

---

### Dialogue 1 — 在家里 zài jiā lǐ (_At home_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|晚上我们去饭馆吃饭，怎么样？|Wǎnshang wǒmen qù fànguǎn chī fàn, zěnmeyàng?|Shall we go to a restaurant tonight, how about it?|
|B|我不想去外面吃，我想在家吃。|Wǒ bù xiǎng qù wàimiàn chī, wǒ xiǎng zài jiā chī.|I don't want to eat out, I want to eat at home.|
|A|那你准备做什么呢？|Nà nǐ zhǔnbèi zuò shénme ne?|Then what are you planning to make?|
|B|就做你爱吃的鱼吧。|Jiù zuò nǐ ài chī de yú ba.|I'll just make the fish you like.|

**Question:** 今天晚上他们在哪儿吃饭？ _(Where are they eating tonight?)_

---

### Dialogue 2 — 在商店 zài shāngdiàn (_In a shop_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|帮我看一下这件衣服怎么样。|Bāng wǒ kàn yíxià zhè jiàn yīfu zěnmeyàng.|Help me see what you think of this garment.|
|B|颜色还可以，就是有点儿大。|Yánsè hái kěyǐ, jiùshì yǒudiǎnr dà.|The colour is okay, it's just a bit big.|
|A|这件小的怎么样？|Zhè jiàn xiǎo de zěnmeyàng?|How about this smaller one?|
|B|这件不错，就买这件吧。|Zhè jiàn búcuò, jiù mǎi zhè jiàn ba.|This one is nice, just get this one.|

**Question:** 她觉得那件衣服怎么样？ _(What does she think of that garment?)_

---

### Dialogue 3 — 在教室 zài jiàoshì (_In the classroom_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|今天去不去打球？|Jīntiān qù bu qù dǎ qiú?|Are we going to play ball today?|
|B|这两天有点儿累，不去打球了。|Zhè liǎng tiān yǒudiǎnr lèi, bú qù dǎ qiú le.|I've been a bit tired these two days, I won't go play.|
|A|你在做什么呢？是在想昨天的考试吗？|Nǐ zài zuò shénme ne? Shì zài xiǎng zuótiān de kǎoshì ma?|What are you doing? Are you thinking about yesterday's exam?|
|B|是啊，我觉得听和说还可以，读和写不好，很多字我都不知道是什么意思。|Shì a, wǒ juéde tīng hé shuō hái kěyǐ, dú hé xiě bù hǎo, hěn duō zì wǒ dōu bù zhīdào shì shénme yìsi.|Yes, I think listening and speaking were okay, but reading and writing not good — many characters I don't even know the meaning of.|

**Questions:** 她今天为什么不去打球了？她觉得昨天的考试怎么样？

---

### Dialogue 4 — 在公司 zài gōngsī (_At the office_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|休息一下吧，喝咖啡吗？|Xiūxi yíxià ba, hē kāfēi ma?|Take a break, want some coffee?|
|B|不喝了，我已经喝两杯了。|Bù hē le, wǒ yǐjīng hē liǎng bēi le.|No thanks, I've already had two cups.|
|A|是啊，咖啡喝多了对身体不好。|Shì a, kāfēi hē duō le duì shēntǐ bù hǎo.|True, drinking too much coffee is bad for your health.|
|B|以后我少喝一点儿，每天喝一杯。|Yǐhòu wǒ shǎo hē yìdiǎnr, měi tiān hē yì bēi.|From now on I'll drink a little less, one cup a day.|

> Useful expressions: 少 shǎo + V = to do less of… · 多 duō + V = to do more of…
>
> 少看电视 shǎo kàn diànshì · 多喝水 duō hē shuǐ · 少吃汉堡 shǎo chī hànbǎo · 多吃水果 duō chī shuǐguǒ

**Question:** 他为什么以后每天就喝一杯咖啡？ _(Why will he drink only one coffee a day from now on?)_

## 🔡 Phonetics

> **Sentence stress (2)**
>
> **(1) 定语重读 — Stressing the attributive modifier**
> The phrase describing a noun is stressed:
> - 那是我妈妈做的中国菜。Nà shì wǒ māma zuò de Zhōngguó cài.
> - 我最喜欢红色的衣服。Wǒ zuì xǐhuan hóngsè de yīfu.
>
> **(2) 状语重读 — Stressing the adverbial modifier**
> The adverbial phrase is stressed:
> - 你的杯子就在那儿。Nǐ de bēizi jiù zài nàr.
> - 大卫的狗飞一样地跑了过去。Dàwèi de gǒu fēi yíyàng de pǎole guòqù.
> - 我爸爸是医生，每天从早到晚忙工作。Wǒ bàba shì yīshēng, měi tiān cóng zǎo dào wǎn máng gōngzuò.

## ✍️ Characters

> **Standalone characters:**
>
> - 鱼 yú: the ancient form looks like a whole fish → means "fish"
> - 衣 yī: the ancient form looks like a traditional Chinese garment → means "clothing"

> **Radicals:**
>
> - 子字旁 (子): related to children → e.g. 孩 hái (child), 孙 sūn (grandson)
> - 广字头 (广): related to buildings → e.g. 店 diàn (shop), 床 chuáng (bed)

## 💡 Quick Recap

|Key point|Structure|Example|
|---|---|---|
|Conclude / decide|就 + verb (+ 吧)|就吃鱼吧 jiù chī yú ba|
|Concession|就是 + complaint|就是有点儿大 jiùshì yǒudiǎnr dà|
|Passable / acceptable|还 + positive adj.|还好 hái hǎo / 还可以 hái kěyǐ|
|A bit (negative)|有点儿 + adj.|有点儿冷 yǒu diǎnr lěng|
|A bit of (quantity)|一点儿 + noun|一点儿水 yì diǎnr shuǐ|
|A little more (comparison)|adj. + 一点儿|便宜一点儿 piányí yì diǎnr|
|Gentle suggestion|sentence + 吧|我们走吧 wǒmen zǒu ba|
|Good/bad for|A 对 B + adj.|运动对身体好 yùndòng duì shēntǐ hǎo|
|Before / after|以前 yǐqián / 以后 yǐhòu|上课以前 / 下课以后|
|Prepare / plan to|准备 + V/N|准备考试 zhǔnbèi kǎoshì|
|Measure word for clothes|件 jiàn|这件衣服 zhè jiàn yīfu|
|Outside / inside|外面 / 里面|去外面吃 qù wàimiàn chī|
|Do less / more of|少/多 + V|少喝咖啡 / 多喝水|
