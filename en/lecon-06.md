
# nǐ zěnme bù chī le

_"Why aren't you eating anymore?"_

---

## 📚 New Vocabulary

---

### 1. 门 — mén — _door, gate (n.)_

> Expressions: 大门 dà mén (main gate) · 小门 xiǎo mén (small door) · 门口 mén kǒu (entrance) · 开门 kāi mén (to open the door) · 关门 guān mén (to close the door)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|门外有一辆自行车。|Mén wài yǒu yí liàng zìxíngchē.|There's a bike outside the door.|
|请你开门。|Qǐng nǐ kāi mén.|Please open the door.|
|商店几点关门？|Shāngdiàn jǐ diǎn guān mén?|What time does the shop close?|

---

### 2. 外 — wài — _outside (n.)_

> Antonym: 里 lǐ = inside
>
> 外面 wài miàn = outside · 里面 lǐ miàn = inside

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|他们在车外。|Tāmen zài chē wài.|They're outside the car.|
|她在车里。|Tā zài chē lǐ.|She's inside the car.|
|门外有一辆自行车。|Mén wài yǒu yí liàng zìxíngchē.|There's a bike outside the door.|

---

### 3. 自行车 — zì xíng chē — _bicycle (n.)_

> Measure word: 辆 liàng → 一辆自行车 yí liàng zìxíngchē = one bicycle
>
> Useful expression: 骑自行车 qí zìxíngchē = to ride a bike

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|门外有一辆自行车。|Mén wài yǒu yí liàng zìxíngchē.|There's a bike outside the door.|
|她们骑自行车。|Tāmen qí zìxíngchē.|They ride bikes.|
|你经常骑自行车吗？|Nǐ jīngcháng qí zìxíngchē ma?|Do you often ride a bike?|

---

### 4. 羊肉 — yáng ròu — _mutton, lamb (n.)_

> 羊 yáng = sheep, goat · 肉 ròu = meat
>
> Types of meat: 鸡肉 jī ròu (chicken) · 牛肉 niú ròu (beef) · 猪肉 zhū ròu (pork) · 鸭肉 yā ròu (duck)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你喜欢吃肉吗？|Nǐ xǐhuan chī ròu ma?|Do you like eating meat?|
|你喜欢吃什么肉？|Nǐ xǐhuan chī shénme ròu?|What kind of meat do you like?|
|这碗羊肉面多少钱？|Zhè wǎn yáng ròu miàn duōshǎo qián?|How much is this bowl of lamb noodles?|

---

### 5. 好吃 — hǎo chī — _delicious, tasty (adj.) [for food]_

> Structure **好 hǎo + Verb** = pleasant to [verb]:
>
> 好喝 hǎo hē (nice to drink) · 好看 hǎo kàn (nice to look at) · 好听 hǎo tīng (nice to listen to) · 好玩 hǎo wán (fun) · 好用 hǎo yòng (handy, easy to use)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|这碗牛肉面很好吃。|Zhè wǎn niúròu miàn hěn hǎochī.|This bowl of beef noodles is very tasty.|
|英国红茶非常好喝。|Yīngguó hóngchá fēicháng hǎohē.|English black tea is really nice to drink.|
|迪士尼乐园很好玩。|Dísīní lèyuán hěn hǎowán.|Disneyland is great fun.|

>
> Types of noodles: 羊肉面 yáng ròu miàn (lamb noodles) · 牛肉面 niú ròu miàn (beef noodles) · 鸡肉面 jī ròu miàn (chicken noodles) · 鸭肉面 yā ròu miàn (duck noodles) · 猪肉面 zhū ròu miàn (pork noodles) · 意大利面 yì dà lì miàn (pasta)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|

|你想吃什么面条？|Nǐ xiǎng chī shénme miàntiáo?|What kind of noodles do you want to eat?|
|你会做意大利面吗？|Nǐ huì zuò yìdàlì miàn ma?|Do you know how to make pasta?|

### 7. 打篮球 — dǎ lán qiú — _to play basketball_

### 8. 因为 — yīn wèi — _because (conj.)_

> Can be used alone or paired with 所以 suǒyǐ.
>
> Structure: Result, 因为 + Reason / 因为 + Cause, 所以 + Effect

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|我在学习中文，因为我想去中国。|Wǒ zài xuéxí Zhōngwén, yīnwèi wǒ xiǎng qù Zhōngguó.|I'm learning Chinese because I want to go to China.|
|今天我们很忙，因为有很多工作。|Jīntiān wǒmen hěn máng, yīnwèi yǒu hěn duō gōngzuò.|We're very busy today because there's a lot of work.|
|因为我很累，所以要休息。|Yīnwèi wǒ hěn lèi, suǒyǐ yào xiūxi.|Since I'm very tired, I'm going to rest.|

---

### 9. 所以 — suǒ yǐ — _so, therefore (conj.)_

> Used alone or paired with 因为 yīnwèi.
>
> Structure: Reason, 所以 + Result

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|汉字太难了，所以我不想学。|Hànzì tài nán le, suǒyǐ wǒ bù xiǎng xué.|Characters are too hard, so I don't want to learn them.|
|我太忙了，所以没有时间给你打电话。|Wǒ tài máng le, suǒyǐ méiyǒu shíjiān gěi nǐ dǎ diànhuà.|I was too busy, so I didn't have time to call you.|
|因为她生病了，所以没去学校。|Yīnwèi tā shēngbìng le, suǒyǐ méi qù xuéxiào.|Because she was ill, she didn't go to school.|

## 🔤 Grammar

---

### 1. The interrogative pronoun 怎么 zěnme

#### _Asking for a reason — surprise or astonishment_

> **Subject + 怎么 + verb/adj.** is used to ask the reason for something, with a nuance of surprise.
>
> More neutral synonym: 为什么 wèi shénme (why)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你怎么不高兴？|Nǐ zěnme bù gāoxìng?|Why aren't you happy?|
|今天怎么这么热？|Jīntiān zěnme zhème rè?|How come it's so hot today?|
|昨天你们怎么都没去打篮球？|Zuótiān nǐmen zěnme dōu méi qù dǎ lánqiú?|How come none of you went to play basketball yesterday?|
|他帮了你，你怎么不说"谢谢"？|Tā bāng le nǐ, nǐ zěnme bù shuō "xièxie"?|He helped you, why don't you say "thank you"?|
|今天是星期一，你怎么不去上班？|Jīntiān shì Xīngqīyī, nǐ zěnme bú qù shàngbān?|It's Monday, why aren't you going to work?|

---

### 2. Reduplication of measure words (AA)

#### _"Each one, every single one"_

> When a measure word is doubled (个个, 件件, 天天, 本本...), it means "each / every".
>
> Followed by **都 dōu** to stress universality.

|Subject|AA|Predicate|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|同学们|个个 gè gè|都很高兴。|The students are every one of them happy.|
|这个商店的衣服|件件 jiàn jiàn|都很漂亮。|Every garment in this shop is beautiful.|
|这个星期我|天天 tiān tiān|都吃羊肉。|This week I eat lamb every single day.|
|这家店的书|本本 běn běn|都很贵。|Every book in this shop is expensive.|
|我的衣服|件件 jiàn jiàn|都很好看。|Every one of my clothes looks nice.|

---

### 3. The conjunction pair 因为……所以……

#### _Expressing cause and effect_

> **因为 yīnwèi** introduces the cause, **所以 suǒyǐ** introduces the effect.
>
> They can be used together or each on its own.

|因为 (Cause)|所以 (Effect)|
|---|---|
|因为她生病了|所以没去学校。|
|因为他每天跑步|所以身体很好。|
|因为昨天下雨|所以我们都没去打篮球。|
|因为太忙|所以我们没有时间吃中饭。|

### Dialogue 1 — 在学校 zài xuéxiào (_At school_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|你知道小王今天什么时候来学校吗？|Nǐ zhīdào Xiǎo Wáng jīntiān shénme shíhòu lái xuéxiào ma?|Do you know when Xiao Wang is coming to school today?|
|B|他已经来了。|Tā yǐjīng lái le.|He's already here.|
|A|你怎么知道他来了？|Nǐ zěnme zhīdào tā lái le?|How do you know he's here?|

**Question:** 小王今天来学校了吗？他看见小王了没有？

|B|这个星期天天都吃羊肉，不想吃了。|Zhège xīngqī tiāntiān dōu chī yángròu, bù xiǎng chī le.|I've eaten lamb every day this week, I don't want any more.|

**Question:** 为什么他今天不想吃羊肉？

## 🔡 Phonetics

> **Logical stress in a sentence**
>
> In a sentence, the information or content the speaker wants to emphasise is stressed. This is logical (emphatic) stress. The same sentence can have different meanings depending on where the stress falls.
>
> Example with 他在房间学习汉语呢。Tā zài fángjiān xuéxí Hànyǔ ne.
> - Stress on **他** → *He* is the one studying Chinese (not someone else).
> - Stress on **房间** → He's studying Chinese *in the room* (not elsewhere).

## ✍️ Characters

> **Standalone characters:**
>
> - 门 mén: the ancient form depicts two door panels → means "door"
> - 羊 yáng: the ancient form looks like a sheep's head seen from the front → means "sheep"

> **Radicals:**
>
> - 犭 (反犬旁): related to animals → e.g. 猫 māo (cat), 狗 gǒu (dog)
> - 心 (心字底): related to mental activities and emotions → e.g. 想 xiǎng (to think), 念 niàn (to think of, to miss)

## 💡 Quick Recap

|Key point|Structure|Example|
|---|---|---|
|Ask the reason (surprise)|Subject + 怎么 + V/adj.|你怎么不吃了？nǐ zěnme bù chī le?|
|Why (neutral)|为什么 wèi shénme|水为什么不热？shuǐ wèishénme bú rè?|
|Cause|因为 yīnwèi + cause|因为下雨 yīnwèi xià yǔ|
|Effect|所以 suǒyǐ + result|所以没去 suǒyǐ méi qù|
|Cause–effect|因为…… 所以……|因为太忙，所以没时间|
|Measure word reduplication|AA + 都 dōu|个个都很高兴 gè gè dōu hěn gāoxìng|
|Often|经常 jīngcháng + V|经常游泳 jīngcháng yóuyǒng|
|Nice to eat/drink|好 hǎo + V|好吃 hǎochī / 好喝 hǎohē|
|Outside / inside|门外 mén wài / 门里 mén lǐ|门外有自行车 mén wài yǒu zìxíngchē|
|Kilogram|公斤 gōngjīn / 斤 jīn|七十公斤 qīshí gōngjīn|
|Family|姐姐/哥哥/弟弟/妹妹|去看他姐姐 qù kàn tā jiějie|
|Meats|X肉 ròu|羊肉/牛肉/鸡肉/猪肉/鸭肉|
|Noodles|X面 miàn|羊肉面/牛肉面/意大利面|
