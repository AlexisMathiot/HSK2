
# nǐ jiā lí gōngsī yuǎn ma

_"Is your home far from your company?"_

---

## 📚 New Vocabulary

---

### 1. 教室 — jiào shì — _classroom (n.)_

> 教 jiào = to teach · 室 shì = room
>
> Useful expressions: 在教室里 zài jiàoshì lǐ (in the classroom) · 上课 shàng kè (to have/go to class)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|他们在教室上课。|Tāmen zài jiàoshì shàngkè.|They're in class in the classroom.|
|你觉得他们的教室怎么样？|Nǐ juéde tāmen de jiàoshì zěnmeyàng?|What do you think of their classroom?|
|这个教室里面有什么？|Zhège jiàoshì lǐmiàn yǒu shénme?|What's in this classroom?|

---

### 2. 机场 — jī chǎng — _airport (n.)_

> 飞机 fēijī = plane · 坐飞机 zuò fēijī = to take a plane
>
> Related words: 城市 chéngshì = city · 如果 rúguǒ = if

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你的城市有机场吗？|Nǐ de chéngshì yǒu jīchǎng ma?|Is there an airport in your city?|
|如果你回国，你在哪个机场坐飞机？|Rúguǒ nǐ huí guó, nǐ zài nǎ ge jīchǎng zuò fēijī?|If you go back to your country, which airport do you fly from?|
|在中国，在机场吃东西很贵。|Zài Zhōngguó, zài jīchǎng chī dōngxi hěn guì.|In China, eating at the airport is expensive.|

---

### 3. 路 — lù — _road, way, route (n.)_

> Useful structure: **去/来/回 …… 的路上 qù/lái/huí … de lùshang** = on the way to / from / back from …
>
> 我在路上 wǒ zài lùshang = I'm on my way / I'm coming

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|这是什么路？|Zhè shì shénme lù?|Which road is this?|
|你最喜欢的饭馆儿在什么路？|Nǐ zuì xǐhuan de fànguǎnr zài shénme lù?|Which road is your favourite restaurant on?|
|来学校的路上，你喜欢听歌吗？|Lái xuéxiào de lùshang, nǐ xǐhuan tīng gē ma?|On the way to school, do you like listening to music?|
|我在路上。|Wǒ zài lùshang.|I'm on my way.|

---

### 4. 离 — lí — _to be away from, at … from (v.)_

> Key structure for expressing distance (place or time):
>
> **Place A + 离 + Place B + distance** → A is X away from B
>
> **Date A + 离 + Date B + 还有 …… hái yǒu** → there is still … between A and B

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你的家离机场多少公里？|Nǐ de jiā lí jīchǎng duōshǎo gōnglǐ?|How many kilometres is your home from the airport?|
|你的家离商店多少米？|Nǐ de jiā lí shāngdiàn duōshǎo mǐ?|How many metres is your home from the shop?|
|今年离2050年还有二十五年。|Jīnnián lí 2050 nián hái yǒu èrshíwǔ nián.|From this year to 2050 there are still 25 years.|
|现在离你的生日还有多长时间？|Xiànzài lí nǐ de shēngrì hái yǒu duō cháng shíjiān?|How long is it from now until your birthday?|

### 5. 公司 — gōng sī — _company, firm (n.)_

> Expressions: 在公司工作 zài gōngsī gōngzuò (to work at a company) · 外国公司 wàiguó gōngsī (foreign company)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你在什么公司工作？|Nǐ zài shénme gōngsī gōngzuò?|Which company do you work for?|

|你在这个公司工作了多长时间？|Nǐ zài zhège gōngsī gōngzuò le duō cháng shíjiān?|How long have you worked at this company?|
|你想在外国公司工作吗？|Nǐ xiǎng zài wàiguó gōngsī gōngzuò ma?|Would you like to work at a foreign company?|

### 6. 远 — yuǎn — _far, distant (adj.)_ ↔ 近 jìn = _near, close_

> Structure: **A 离 lí B + 远/近** = A is far/near from B
>
> 多远 duō yuǎn ? = how far?

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|美国离中国远吗？|Měiguó lí Zhōngguó yuǎn ma?|Is the US far from China?|
|美国离中国很远。|Měiguó lí Zhōngguó hěn yuǎn.|The US is very far from China.|
|你的公司离你的家多远？|Nǐ de gōngsī lí nǐ de jiā duō yuǎn?|How far is your company from your home?|
|我的家离学校不远。|Wǒ de jiā lí xuéxiào bù yuǎn.|My home isn't far from the school.|
|深圳离香港很近。|Shēnzhèn lí Xiānggǎng hěn jìn.|Shenzhen is very close to Hong Kong.|

### 8. 小时 — xiǎo shí — _hour (duration) (n.)_

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|

|一小时前你在做什么？|Yì xiǎoshí qián nǐ zài zuò shénme?|What were you doing an hour ago?|

## 🔤 Grammar

---

### 1. The modal adverb 还 hái

#### _Continuity of an action or state_

> **Subject + 还 + verb / adj.** = "still"
>
> Negative form: **Subject + 还 + 不/没 + verb / adj.** = "not yet"

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|二十分钟后我们还在上课。|Èrshí fēnzhōng hòu wǒmen hái zài shàngkè.|In 20 minutes we'll still be in class.|
|去年我想去法国，今年我还想去法国。|Qùnián wǒ xiǎng qù Fǎguó, jīnnián wǒ hái xiǎng qù Fǎguó.|Last year I wanted to go to France, this year I still want to.|
|明天还很热。|Míngtiān hái hěn rè.|Tomorrow it'll still be very hot.|
|他还没起床。|Tā hái méi qǐchuáng.|He hasn't got up yet.|
|她还不累。|Tā hái bú lèi.|She isn't tired yet.|
|八点了，他还在睡觉。|Bā diǎn le, tā hái zài shuìjiào.|It's 8 o'clock and he's still asleep.|
|你怎么还没吃饭？|Nǐ zěnme hái méi chī fàn?|How come you haven't eaten yet?|

---

### 2. The time adverb 就 jiù

#### _"Already, as early as, in just…" — emphasis on speed or earliness_

> **Subject + Time + 就 + verb + (了)** = "X was already doing the action at that (early) time"
>
> **Subject + Duration + 就 + verb + (了)** = "X did the action in just that (short) time"

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|她五点就起床了。|Tā wǔ diǎn jiù qǐchuáng le.|She got up as early as 5.|
|她六点就吃早饭了。|Tā liù diǎn jiù chī zǎofàn le.|She'd already had breakfast by 6.|
|她三岁就会写字了。|Tā sān suì jiù huì xiě zì le.|She could already write at the age of 3.|
|他们二十岁就结婚了。|Tāmen èrshí suì jiù jiéhūn le.|They got married as early as 20.|
|坐飞机两小时就到了。|Zuò fēijī liǎng xiǎoshí jiù dào le.|By plane you're there in just 2 hours.|
|我走路十分钟就到了。|Wǒ zǒulù shí fēnzhōng jiù dào le.|On foot I'm there in just 10 minutes.|

---

### 3. The verb 离 lí — expressing distance

> **A + 离 + B + (distance)**: indicates the distance to a place, a moment, or a goal.

|A|离|B|……|
|---|---|---|---|
|我家|离|学校|很远。|
|学校|离|机场|有 20 多公里。|
|（今天）|离|我的生日|还有一个多星期呢！|

---

### 4. The modal particle 呢 ne

#### _Confirming a fact with a nuance of insistence or exaggeration_

> Placed at the end of a declarative sentence (or after a verbal/adjectival predicate), it serves to **persuade**, **insist**, or **exaggerate**.

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|八点上课，时间还早呢。|Bā diǎn shàng kè, shíjiān hái zǎo ne.|Class is at 8, there's still plenty of time!|
|医院离我们这儿还远呢。|Yīyuàn lí wǒmen zhèr hái yuǎn ne.|The hospital is still far from here!|
|坐公共汽车要一个多小时呢！|Zuò gōnggòng qìchē yào yí ge duō xiǎoshí ne!|The bus takes more than an hour!|
|现在四十度呢。|Xiànzài sì shí dù ne.|It's 40 degrees right now!|
|这个八万七千块呢。|Zhège bā wàn qī qiān kuài ne.|This costs 87,000 yuan!|

> **The 4 uses of 呢 ne:**
>
> 1. Confirm a fact with exaggeration → 这件衣服九千块呢。
> 2. Bounce a question to someone else ("And you?") → 我走路去。你呢？
> 3. Indicate continuity of an action/state → 我正去机场呢。
> 4. Ask where something is → 我的小猫呢？

### Dialogue 1 — 在家里 zài jiā lǐ (_At home_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|大卫回来了吗？|Dàwèi huílai le ma?|Is David back?|
|B|没有，他还在教室学习呢。|Méiyǒu, tā hái zài jiàoshì xuéxí ne.|No, he's still studying in the classroom.|
|A|已经九点多了，他怎么还在学习？|Yǐjīng jiǔ diǎn duō le, tā zěnme hái zài xuéxí?|It's already past 9, how come he's still studying?|
|B|明天有考试，他说今天要好好准备。|Míngtiān yǒu kǎoshì, tā shuō jīntiān yào hǎohāo zhǔnbèi.|There's an exam tomorrow, he said he wants to prepare well today.|

> **好好 hǎohāo** = adv. "properly, to the fullest" → 好好学习 hǎohāo xuéxí (to study hard) · 好好想想 hǎohāo xiǎngxiang (to think it over carefully)
>
> **Question:** 大卫在哪儿学习呢？九点多了，大卫为什么还不休息？

## 🔡 Phonetics

> **Basic sentence intonations**
>
> There are two basic intonations: **rising ↗** and **falling ↘**.
>
> - **Question** sentence → rising intonation ↗
> - **Declarative** sentence → falling intonation ↘
>
> Examples:
>
> - 他姓张？↗ (Is his surname Zhang?) / 他姓张。↘ (His surname is Zhang.)
> - 他不喜欢喝啤酒？↗ (He doesn't like beer?) / 他不喜欢喝啤酒。↘ (He doesn't like beer.)

## ✍️ Characters

> **Radicals:**
>
> - 彳 (双人旁 _shuāng rén páng_, "double person"): generally related to the idea of **walking / going** → e.g. 行 xíng (to walk), 往 wǎng (to go towards)
> - 攵 (反文旁 _fǎn wén páng_): generally related to the idea of **hitting / striking** → e.g. 放 fàng (to release), 收 shōu (to receive, to accept)

## 💡 Quick Recap

|Key point|Structure|Example|
|---|---|---|
|Continuity (still)|还 hái + V/adj.|他还在学习 tā hái zài xuéxí|
|Not yet|还 hái + 不/没 + V/adj.|他还没起床 tā hái méi qǐchuáng|
|Early / quickly|Subject + time/duration + 就 jiù + V + (了)|二十分钟就到了 èrshí fēnzhōng jiù dào le|
|Distance (place)|A 离 lí B + 远/近/distance|我家离公司很远 wǒ jiā lí gōngsī hěn yuǎn|
|Distance (time)|A 离 lí B + 还有 hái yǒu …|离生日还有一个星期 lí shēngrì hái yǒu yí ge xīngqī|
|Insistence / exaggeration|… 呢 ne|要一个多小时呢 yào yí ge duō xiǎoshí ne|
|On the way to …|去/来/回 …… 的路上 de lùshang|去机场的路上 qù jīchǎng de lùshang|
|Hour (duration)|小时 xiǎoshí|两个小时 liǎng ge xiǎoshí|
|Hour (clock)|点 diǎn|两点 liǎng diǎn|
|Far / near|远 yuǎn / 近 jìn|很远 hěn yuǎn / 很近 hěn jìn|
|Fast / slow|快 kuài / 慢 màn|开车快 kāichē kuài / 走路慢 zǒulù màn|
|Arrive|到 dào|我到了 wǒ dào le|
|Walk|走 zǒu / 走路 zǒulù|走去学校 zǒu qù xuéxiào|
|Celebrate|过 guò + holiday|过生日 / 过新年 / 过圣诞|
|Transport|公共汽车 / 出租车 / 飞机 / 自行车|坐公共汽车 zuò gōnggòng qìchē|
