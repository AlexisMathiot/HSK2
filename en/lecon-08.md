
# ràng wǒ xiǎng xiang zài gàosu nǐ

_"Let me think about it and I'll tell you (later)."_

---

## 📚 New Vocabulary

---

### 1. 再 — zài — _again, once more (adv.)_

> ⚠️ **再 ≠ 又**: 再 indicates a **future** or **planned** repetition, while 又 yòu indicates a **past** repetition.
>
> Structure: **再 (zài) + Verb** = "to do the action again / once more"

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|我2008年去了北京，我想今年再去一次。|Wǒ 2008 nián qù le Běijīng, wǒ xiǎng jīnnián zài qù yí cì.|I went to Beijing in 2008, I'd like to go again this year.|
|十分钟前他喝了一杯啤酒，他想再喝一杯。|Shí fēnzhōng qián tā hē le yì bēi píjiǔ, tā xiǎng zài hē yì bēi.|Ten minutes ago he had a beer, he wants to have one more.|
|再说一次。|Zài shuō yí cì.|Say it once more.|
|再听一次。|Zài tīng yí cì.|Listen once more.|

---

### 2. 让 — ràng — _to let, to allow, to ask someone to (v.)_

> A "pivot" verb: the person who receives the action becomes the subject of the next verb.
>
> **Structure: Subject + 让 (ràng) / 叫 (jiào) + Object/Subject + V + (Object)**
>
> Three nuances: **to let, to allow, to ask (someone to do)**.

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|老师让学生看一下。|Lǎoshī ràng xuéshēng kàn yí xià.|The teacher asks the students to take a look.|
|老师让学生回答问题。|Lǎoshī ràng xuéshēng huídá wèntí.|The teacher asks the students to answer questions.|
|妈妈让他做作业。|Māma ràng tā zuò zuòyè.|Mum tells him to do his homework.|
|上课的时候，老师让你用手机吗？|Shàngkè de shíhou, lǎoshī ràng nǐ yòng shǒujī ma?|During class, does the teacher let you use your phone?|
|你的老板让你做很多工作吗？|Nǐ de lǎobǎn ràng nǐ zuò hěn duō gōngzuò ma?|Does your boss make you do a lot of work?|

---

### 3. 告诉 — gào sù — _to tell, to inform (v.)_

> ⚠️ Different from 说 shuō: **告诉** always introduces **a person** (the one being told).
>
> **Structure: Subject + 告诉 + someone + (thing)**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你可以告诉我你的中文名字吗？|Nǐ kěyǐ gàosù wǒ nǐ de zhōngwén míngzi ma?|Can you tell me your Chinese name?|
|请告诉我你生日是几月几号。|Qǐng gàosù wǒ nǐ shēngrì shì jǐ yuè jǐ hào.|Please tell me what date your birthday is.|
|你能告诉我现在几点了吗？|Nǐ néng gàosù wǒ xiànzài jǐ diǎn le ma?|Can you tell me what time it is?|

---

### 4. 等 — děng — _to wait (v.)_

> Useful expression: **请等一下 qǐng děng yí xià** = "please wait a moment"

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|请等一下。|Qǐng děng yí xià.|Please wait a moment.|
|你在这里等谁？|Nǐ zài zhèlǐ děng shéi?|Who are you waiting for here?|
|我在等女朋友呢。|Wǒ zài děng nǚpéngyou ne.|I'm waiting for my girlfriend.|
|你等多长时间了？|Nǐ děng duō cháng shíjiān le?|How long have you been waiting?|
|已经等了半个小时了。|Yǐjīng děng le bàn ge xiǎoshí le.|I've already been waiting for half an hour.|

---

### 5. 找 — zhǎo — _to look for (v.)_

> 找 zhǎo = to look for · **找到 zhǎo dào** = to find (result achieved)
>
> Also used for "to give change" (找钱 zhǎo qián) or "to seek someone for something" (找你有事 zhǎo nǐ yǒu shì).

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你觉得在中国找工作难吗？|Nǐ juéde zài Zhōngguó zhǎo gōngzuò nán ma?|Do you think it's hard to find a job in China?|
|你在找什么？|Nǐ zài zhǎo shénme?|What are you looking for?|
|我在找我的手机呢。|Wǒ zài zhǎo wǒ de shǒujī ne.|I'm looking for my phone.|
|你找到手机了吗？|Nǐ zhǎo dào shǒujī le ma?|Did you find your phone?|
|找到了，手机在桌子上呢。|Zhǎo dào le, shǒujī zài zhuōzi shàng ne.|Found it, the phone is on the table.|

---

### 6. 事情 — shì qing — _thing, matter, affair (n.)_

> Expression: **有什么事情？yǒu shénme shìqing?** = "what's the matter / what's it about?"
>
> **找你有事情** zhǎo nǐ yǒu shìqing = "I have something to ask/tell you"

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你找我有什么事情？|Nǐ zhǎo wǒ yǒu shénme shìqing?|What do you want from me? / What are you looking for me about?|
|这个星期五晚上你有时间吗？|Zhè ge xīngqīwǔ wǎnshang nǐ yǒu shíjiān ma?|Do you have time this Friday evening?|
|麦克打电话给小明有什么事情？|MàiKè dǎ diànhuà gěi Xiǎomíng yǒu shénme shìqing?|Why is Mike calling Xiao Ming?|

---

### 7. 服务员 — fú wù yuán — _waiter, waitress, attendant (n.)_

> Very common in restaurants or hotels to call the staff.

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|服务员，请给我菜单。|Fúwùyuán, qǐng gěi wǒ càidān.|Waiter, please give me the menu.|
|服务员，请点菜。|Fúwùyuán, qǐng diǎn cài.|Waiter, I'd like to order.|
|服务员，买单。|Fúwùyuán, mǎi dān.|Waiter, the bill.|
|好的，一共二百八十块。|Hǎo de, yí gòng èr bǎi bā shí kuài.|Sure, that's 280 yuan in total.|

---

### 8. 白 — bái — _white (adj.)_

> Expressions: 白茶 bái chá (white tea) · 白葡萄酒 bái pútáo jiǔ (white wine) · 白色 bái sè (the colour white)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|服务员的衣服是什么颜色的？|Fúwùyuán de yīfu shì shénme yánsè de?|What colour are the waiter's clothes?|
|是白的。/ 是白色的。|Shì bái de. / Shì bái sè de.|They're white.|
|你喜欢喝红酒还是白葡萄酒？|Nǐ xǐhuan hē hóngjiǔ háishì bái pútáo jiǔ?|Do you prefer red wine or white wine?|
|你觉得白茶好喝吗？|Nǐ juéde bái chá hǎo hē ma?|Do you think white tea is nice to drink?|

---

### 9. 黑 — hēi — _black (adj.)_

> 黑色 hēi sè = the colour black · 黑猫 hēi māo = black cat

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|今天服务员的衣服是白的还是黑的？|Jīntiān fúwùyuán de yīfu shì bái de háishì hēi de?|Today are the waiter's clothes white or black?|
|这双黑色的鞋子多少钱？|Zhè shuāng hēi sè de xiézi duōshǎo qián?|How much is this pair of black shoes?|
|这只猫是黑的吗？|Zhè zhī māo shì hēi de ma?|Is this cat black?|

---

### 10. 贵 — guì — _expensive (adj.)_   ↔ 便宜 pián yi — _cheap_

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你觉得这件衣服贵吗？|Nǐ juéde zhè jiàn yīfu guì ma?|Do you think this garment is expensive?|
|在中国坐公共汽车很便宜，在你的国家呢？|Zài Zhōngguó zuò gōnggòng qìchē hěn piányi, zài nǐ de guójiā ne?|In China the bus is cheap. What about in your country?|
|你觉得白色的狗贵一点儿还是黑色的狗贵一点儿？|Nǐ juéde báisè de gǒu guì yì diǎnr háishì hēisè de gǒu guì yì diǎnr?|Do you think white dogs are a bit more expensive, or black ones?|

## 🔤 Grammar

---

### 1. The question "……，好吗？(hǎo ma)"

> Used to ask another person's opinion or agreement (≈ "is that okay? alright?")
>
> **Declarative sentence + 好吗？**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|我们一起去吃饭，好吗？|Wǒmen yìqǐ qù chī fàn, hǎo ma?|Let's go eat together, okay?|
|你明天下午给我打电话，好吗？|Nǐ míngtiān xiàwǔ gěi wǒ dǎ diànhuà, hǎo ma?|Call me tomorrow afternoon, alright?|
|我们下午去看电影，好吗？|Wǒmen xiàwǔ qù kàn diànyǐng, hǎo ma?|Let's go to the cinema this afternoon, okay?|

---

### 2. The adverb 再 zài — _future repetition / continuation_

> Indicates **repetition or continuation** of an action or state, or that the action will happen **under certain circumstances** (after a delay, after another event).
>
> **Structure: Subject + 再 (zài) + V (+O)**

|Meaning|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|once more|你再看看这本书吧。|Nǐ zài kànkan zhè běn shū ba.|Read this book again.|
|after a delay|（明天）再给我打电话吧。|(Míngtiān) zài gěi wǒ dǎ diànhuà ba.|Call me back (tomorrow).|
|after another action|让我想想再告诉你。|Ràng wǒ xiǎng xiang zài gàosù nǐ.|Let me think, then I'll tell you.|
|"another / more"|服务员，再给我两瓶啤酒。|Fúwùyuán, zài gěi wǒ liǎng píng píjiǔ.|Waiter, two more beers please.|

> **Mixed examples**:
>
> - 这个地方很好玩儿，明年我想再来。 → _again / once more_
> - 今天张经理不在，这件事情我们明天再说吧。 → _after a delay_
> - 我吃了饭再告诉你。 → _after another action_
> - 不好意思，请再等我十分钟。 → _another / some more_

---

### 3. The pivot sentence (兼语句 jiānyǔjù)

> The predicate is made of **two verbs**: the object of the 1st verb is the subject of the 2nd verb. The 1st verb is often **causative**: 请 qǐng (to invite), 让 ràng (to let), 叫 jiào (to ask).
>
> **Structure: Subject + V₁ (请/让/叫) + Object/Subject + V₂ (+O)**

|Subject|V₁|O / S|Predicate|
|---|---|---|---|
|我|请|你|吃饭。|
|你|让|我|再想想。|
|我|叫|人|去看看。|
|老师|让|我|写汉字。|

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你喜欢请朋友来你家吗？|Nǐ xǐhuan qǐng péngyou lái nǐ jiā ma?|Do you like inviting friends to your home?|
|你生日的时候，朋友请你吃饭吗？|Nǐ shēngrì de shíhou, péngyou qǐng nǐ chī fàn ma?|On your birthday, do your friends treat you to a meal?|
|你的老板经常叫你周末工作吗？|Nǐ de lǎobǎn jīngcháng jiào nǐ zhōumò gōngzuò ma?|Does your boss often ask you to work on weekends?|
|你经常叫你的中国朋友帮你在淘宝上买东西吗？|Nǐ jīngcháng jiào nǐ de Zhōngguó péngyou bāng nǐ zài Táobǎo shàng mǎi dōngxi ma?|Do you often ask your Chinese friends to help you buy things on Taobao?|

---

### 4. Verb reduplication (动词重叠)

> The reduplicated form of a verb indicates **a short time, a small amount, a slight degree, or an attempt**. It conveys a **relaxed, casual** tone and is very common in speech.

#### Monosyllabic verbs

|A|AA|A 一 A|
|---|---|---|
|说 shuō (to say)|说说 shuōshuo|说一说 shuō yi shuō|
|听 tīng (to listen)|听听 tīngting|听一听 tīng yi tīng|
|看 kàn (to look)|看看 kànkan|看一看 kàn yi kàn|

#### Disyllabic verbs

|AB|ABAB|
|---|---|
|学习 xuéxí|学习学习 xuéxí xuéxí|
|准备 zhǔnbèi|准备准备 zhǔnbèi zhǔnbèi|
|运动 yùndòng|运动运动 yùndòng yùndòng|

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|我累了，我想休息休息。|Wǒ lèi le, wǒ xiǎng xiūxi xiūxi.|I'm tired, I want to rest a little.|
|这个菜很好吃，你尝尝。|Zhè ge cài hěn hǎochī, nǐ chángchang.|This dish is tasty, have a little taste.|
|你的新手机真好看，我能看一看吗？|Nǐ de xīn shǒujī zhēn hǎokàn, wǒ néng kàn yi kàn ma?|Your new phone looks great, can I have a look?|
|这首歌非常好听，你想不想听一听？|Zhè shǒu gē fēicháng hǎotīng, nǐ xiǎng bu xiǎng tīng yi tīng?|This song is lovely, do you want to give it a listen?|

## 💬 Dialogues

---

### Dialogue 1 — 在教室 zài jiàoshì (_In the classroom_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|我们下午去看电影，好吗？|Wǒmen xiàwǔ qù kàn diànyǐng, hǎo ma?|Let's go to the cinema this afternoon, okay?|
|B|今天下午我没有时间，明天下午再去吧。|Jīntiān xiàwǔ wǒ méiyǒu shíjiān, míngtiān xiàwǔ zài qù ba.|I don't have time this afternoon, let's go tomorrow afternoon instead.|
|A|你想看什么电影？|Nǐ xiǎng kàn shénme diànyǐng?|What film do you want to watch?|
|B|让我想想再告诉你。|Ràng wǒ xiǎng xiang zài gàosù nǐ.|Let me think about it and I'll tell you.|

> **Question:** 他们为什么今天下午不去看电影？

---

### Dialogue 2 — 在宿舍 zài sùshè (_In the dorm_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|外边天气很好，我们出去运动运动吧！|Wàibian tiānqì hěn hǎo, wǒmen chūqù yùndòng yùndòng ba!|The weather is nice outside, let's go exercise a bit!|
|B|你等等我，好吗？王老师让我给大卫打个电话。|Nǐ děngdeng wǒ, hǎo ma? Wáng lǎoshī ràng wǒ gěi Dàwèi dǎ ge diànhuà.|Wait for me, okay? Teacher Wang asked me to call David.|
|A|回来再打吧。找大卫有什么事情吗？|Huílái zài dǎ ba. Zhǎo Dàwèi yǒu shénme shìqing ma?|Call him later when we're back. What do you need David for?|
|B|听说大卫病了，我想找时间去看看他。|Tīngshuō Dàwèi bìng le, wǒ xiǎng zhǎo shíjiān qù kànkan tā.|I heard David is ill, I want to find time to go see him.|

> **Questions:** 王老师为什么让他给大卫打电话？大卫怎么了？

---

### Dialogue 3 — 在宾馆的前台 zài bīnguǎn de qiántái (_At the hotel front desk_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|服务员，我房间的门打不开了。|Fúwùyuán, wǒ fángjiān de mén dǎ bu kāi le.|Excuse me, I can't open the door to my room.|
|B|您住哪个房间？|Nín zhù nǎ ge fángjiān?|Which room are you staying in?|
|A|317 (sān yāo qī)。|Sān yāo qī.|317.|
|B|好的，我叫人去看看。|Hǎo de, wǒ jiào rén qù kànkan.|Okay, I'll have someone go take a look.|

> **Vocabulary:**
>
> - 打不开 dǎ bu kāi = can't be opened / I can't open it
> - 打得开 dǎ de kāi = can be opened / I can open it
>
> **Question:** 她为什么去找服务员？

---

### Dialogue 4 — 在商店 zài shāngdiàn (_In a shop_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|你看看这几件衣服怎么样。|Nǐ kànkan zhè jǐ jiàn yīfu zěnmeyàng.|Take a look at these clothes, what do you think?|
|B|这件白的有点儿长，那件黑的有点儿贵。|Zhè jiàn bái de yǒudiǎnr cháng, nà jiàn hēi de yǒudiǎnr guì.|The white one is a bit long, and the black one is a bit expensive.|
|A|这件红的呢？这是今天新来的。|Zhè jiàn hóng de ne? Zhè shì jīntiān xīn lái de.|How about the red one? It just came in today.|
|B|让我再看看。|Ràng wǒ zài kànkan.|Let me have another look.|

> **Question:** 她为什么不喜欢那件黑的？

## 🔡 Phonetics

> **Declarative sentence intonation**
>
> Declarative sentences in Chinese generally have a **falling intonation ↘**.
>
> Examples:
>
> - 我学习汉语。↘ (I study Chinese.)
> - 他是我的老师。↘ (He is my teacher.)
> - 外边天气很好。↘ (The weather outside is nice.)

---

## ✍️ Characters

> **Radicals:**
>
> - **又** (_yòu zì páng_): various meanings, no single sense. → e.g. 欢 huān (joyful), 对 duì (correct)
> - **巾** (_jīn zì dǐ_): generally related to **cloth, cotton, or textiles**. → e.g. 帮 bāng (to help), 帽 mào (hat)

## 💡 Quick Recap

|Key point|Structure|Example|
|---|---|---|
|Ask for agreement|... 好吗？|我们去吃饭，好吗？|
|Do again / continue later|再 zài + V|再说一次 / 明天再去|
|Pivot sentence|S + 让/请/叫 + O/S + V|老师让我写汉字|
|Verb reduplication (1 syllable)|AA / A 一 A|看看 / 看一看|
|Verb reduplication (2 syllables)|ABAB|学习学习 / 运动运动|
|Tell someone|告诉 + someone + thing|告诉我你的名字|
|Look for / find|找 → 找到|我找到手机了|
|Wait|等 + (someone) + (duration)|等一下 / 等了半个小时|
|Need someone for something|找 + someone + 有事情|你找我有什么事情？|
|Colours|白 / 黑 / 红 / 白色 / 黑色|白色的衣服|
|Expensive / cheap|贵 ↔ 便宜|这件衣服很贵|
|Can / can't + result|V + 得/不 + result|打得开 / 打不开|
|Restaurant|服务员 / 菜单 / 点菜 / 买单|服务员，买单！|
