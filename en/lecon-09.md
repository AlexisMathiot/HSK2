
# tí tài duō, wǒ méi zuò wán

_"There are too many questions, I didn't finish them all."_

---

## 📚 New Vocabulary

---

### 1. 错 — cuò — _wrong, incorrect (adj.)_   ↔ 对 duì — _correct_

> Used as a **resultative complement** after a verb: to do something **wrongly**.
>
> **Structure: V + 对/错 + (O) + 了** · Negation: **没 + V + 对/错 + (O)**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|"1+1=3"，对还是错？|"1+1=3", duì háishì cuò?|"1+1=3", right or wrong?|
|老师写错了。/ 老师没写对。|Lǎoshī xiě cuò le. / Lǎoshī méi xiě duì.|The teacher wrote it wrong. / The teacher didn't write it correctly.|
|老师读对你的名字了吗？|Lǎoshī dú duì nǐ de míngzi le ma?|Did the teacher pronounce your name correctly?|
|上次的作业你都做对了吗？|Shàngcì de zuòyè nǐ dōu zuò duì le ma?|Did you get all of last time's homework right?|
|你打错电话了。|Nǐ dǎ cuò diànhuà le.|You dialled the wrong number.|

---

### 2. 从 — cóng — _from, starting from (prep.)_

> Often paired: **从……到…… cóng...dào...** = "from... to..." (time, distance, process).
>
> **从 + starting point (+ 到 + end point)**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|从早上到晚上|cóng zǎoshang dào wǎnshang|from morning to evening|
|从星期一到星期五|cóng xīngqī yī dào xīngqī wǔ|from Monday to Friday|
|从广州到北京|cóng Guǎngzhōu dào Běijīng|from Guangzhou to Beijing|
|从现在开始|cóng xiànzài kāishǐ|starting from now|
|从你家到学校要多长时间？|Cóng nǐ jiā dào xuéxiào yào duō cháng shíjiān?|How long does it take from your home to school?|
|你是从什么时候开始学中文的？|Nǐ shì cóng shénme shíhou kāishǐ xué zhōngwén de?|Since when did you start learning Chinese?|

### 3. 跳舞 — tiào wǔ — _to dance (v.)_

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你会跳舞吗？|Nǐ huì tiàowǔ ma?|Can you dance?|
|妈妈觉得跳舞对身体好，所以让小丽去学跳舞。|Māma juéde tiàowǔ duì shēntǐ hǎo, suǒyǐ ràng XiǎoLì qù xué tiàowǔ.|Mum thinks dancing is good for your health, so she sends Xiaoli to learn dance.|
|小丽从七岁开始学跳舞，已经跳了十五年了。|XiǎoLì cóng qī suì kāishǐ xué tiàowǔ, yǐjing tiào le shíwǔ nián le.|Xiaoli started learning dance at 7, she's been dancing for 15 years now.|
|如果你有女儿，你想让她学跳舞吗？|Rúguǒ nǐ yǒu nǚ'ér, nǐ xiǎng ràng tā xué tiàowǔ ma?|If you had a daughter, would you want her to learn dance?|

### 4. 第一 — dì yī — _first (ordinal num.)_

> **第 (dì) + Numeral + Measure word** = ordinal number (the 1st, the 2nd...).
>
> With 几: **第几……？dì jǐ...?** = "which one (in order)...?"

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|第一个 / 第二个 / 第几个？|dì yī ge / dì èr ge / dì jǐ ge?|the first / the second / which one (in order)?|
|第一次 / 第一课 / 第一页|dì yī cì / dì yī kè / dì yī yè|the first time / lesson 1 / page 1|
|我们在学第几课？|Wǒmen zài xué dì jǐ kè?|Which lesson are we studying?|
|你第一次来中国是什么时候？|Nǐ dì yī cì lái Zhōngguó shì shénme shíhou?|When was your first time coming to China?|
|今天是这个月的第几天？|Jīntiān shì zhè ge yuè de dì jǐ tiān?|What day of the month is it today?|

### 5. 希望 — xī wàng — _to hope, to wish (v.)_

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你希望今年去哪里旅游？|Nǐ xīwàng jīnnián qù nǎli lǚyóu?|Where do you hope to travel this year?|
|你希望以后住在哪里？|Nǐ xīwàng yǐhòu zhù zài nǎli?|Where do you hope to live in the future?|
|你希望再去一次上海吗？|Nǐ xīwàng zài qù yí cì Shànghǎi ma?|Do you hope to go to Shanghai again?|
|如果你有很多钱，你希望做什么？|Rúguǒ nǐ yǒu hěn duō qián, nǐ xīwàng zuò shénme?|If you had a lot of money, what would you want to do?|

### 6. 问题 — wèn tí — _question; problem (n.)_

> Two meanings: **question** (that you ask) and **problem** (difficulty).
>
> Expression: **没问题！méi wèntí!** = "no problem!"

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你喜欢问老师问题吗？|Nǐ xǐhuan wèn lǎoshī wèntí ma?|Do you like asking the teacher questions?|
|今天的汉语课你有什么问题？|Jīntiān de hànyǔ kè nǐ yǒu shénme wèntí?|What questions do you have about today's Chinese class?|
|她的工作有很多问题。|Tā de gōngzuò yǒu hěn duō wèntí.|Her job has a lot of problems.|
|没问题！|Méi wèntí!|No problem!|

### 7. 欢迎 — huān yíng — _to welcome (v.)_

> **欢迎 + 来/去 + place** = "welcome to..."
>
> In a shop / restaurant: **欢迎光临！huānyíng guānglín!**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|欢迎来中国。|Huānyíng lái Zhōngguó.|Welcome to China.|
|欢迎来我家。|Huānyíng lái wǒ jiā.|Welcome to my home.|
|欢迎去北京。|Huānyíng qù Běijīng.|Welcome to Beijing.|
|欢迎光临！|Huānyíng guānglín!|Welcome! (shop, restaurant...)|

### 8. 上班 — shàng bān — _to go to work (v.)_   ↔ 下班 xiàbān — _to finish work_

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你每天都上班吗？|Nǐ měitiān dōu shàngbān ma?|Do you go to work every day?|
|不是每天，从星期二到星期日上班，星期一我休息。|Bú shì měitiān, cóng xīngqī èr dào xīngqī rì shàngbān, xīngqī yī wǒ xiūxi.|Not every day: I work from Tuesday to Sunday, and rest on Monday.|
|你几点开始上班，几点下班？|Nǐ jǐ diǎn kāishǐ shàngbān, jǐ diǎn xiàbān?|What time do you start work, and what time do you finish?|
|上午七点上班，晚上五点下班。|Shàngwǔ qī diǎn shàngbān, wǎnshang wǔ diǎn xiàbān.|I start at 7 am and finish at 5 pm.|
|今天下班后，你想做什么？|Jīntiān xiàbān hòu, nǐ xiǎng zuò shénme?|After work today, what do you want to do?|

---

### 9. 懂 — dǒng — _to understand (v.)_

> Very common as a **resultative complement**: **听懂** (to understand by listening), **看懂** (to understand by reading/watching).
>
> **V + 懂 + (O) + 了** → negation: **没 + V + 懂 + (O)**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你懂广东话吗？|Nǐ dǒng Guǎngdōng huà ma?|Do you understand Cantonese?|
|今天的课，你都懂了吗？|Jīntiān de kè, nǐ dōu dǒng le ma?|Did you understand all of today's class?|
|这个汉字，你们看懂了吗？|Zhè ge hànzì, nǐmen kàn dǒng le ma?|This character — did you understand it (by reading)?|
|老师说了什么？大家听懂了吗？|Lǎoshī shuō le shénme? Dàjiā tīng dǒng le ma?|What did the teacher say? Did everyone understand?|
|看懂了 ↔ 没看懂 · 听懂了 ↔ 没听懂|kàn dǒng le ↔ méi kàn dǒng · tīng dǒng le ↔ méi tīng dǒng|understood (read) ↔ didn't understand · understood (heard) ↔ didn't understand|

---

### 10. 完 — wán — _to finish, to complete (v.)_

> The most common resultative complement: **V + 完** = "to finish V-ing".
>
> **V + 完 + (O) + 了** → negation: **没 + V + 完 + (O)**

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|她吃完西瓜了吗？|Tā chī wán xīguā le ma?|Has she finished eating the watermelon?|
|她没吃完西瓜。|Tā méi chī wán xīguā.|She hasn't finished the watermelon.|
|今天的中文课，你上完了吗？|Jīntiān de zhōngwén kè, nǐ shàng wán le ma?|Have you finished today's Chinese class?|
|你做完上次的作业了吗？|Nǐ zuò wán shàngcì de zuòyè le ma?|Have you finished last time's homework?|
|做完了 / 没做完 · 喝完了 / 没喝完|zuò wán le / méi zuò wán · hē wán le / méi hē wán|finished doing / not finished · finished drinking / not finished|

---

### 11. 题 — tí — _question, exercise (n.)_

> ⚠️ 题 = exercise/exam question; 问题 = a question you ask or a problem.

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|这里一共有多少题？|Zhèli yígòng yǒu duōshǎo tí?|How many questions are there here in total?|
|这些题难吗？|Zhè xiē tí nán ma?|Are these exercises difficult?|
|这些题你都做完了吗？|Zhè xiē tí nǐ dōu zuò wán le ma?|Have you finished all these exercises?|
|第几题你觉得最难？|Dì jǐ tí nǐ juéde zuì nán?|Which question do you find the hardest?|
|题太多，我没做完。|Tí tài duō, wǒ méi zuò wán.|There are too many questions, I didn't finish them all.|

## 🔤 Grammar

---

### 1. The resultative complement (结果补语)

> A **verb or adjective** placed right after the main verb specifies **the result** of the action.
>
> **Structure: S + V + resultative complement + (O) + 了**
>
> ⚠️ **Negation: 没(有) + V + complement** — and in this case **了 disappears**!
>
> **Question: ……了没有？ le méi yǒu?** at the end of the sentence (or 吗).

|Complement|Meaning|Examples|
|---|---|---|
|完 wán|action finished|做完作业 (to finish homework) · 喝完咖啡 (to finish the coffee)|
|对 duì|done correctly|写对汉字 (to write the character correctly) · 说对名字 (to say the name correctly)|
|错 cuò|done wrongly|打错电话 (wrong number) · 买错衣服 (to buy the wrong garment)|
|懂 dǒng|understood|听懂中文 (to understand spoken Chinese) · 看懂电影 (to understand the film)|
|好 hǎo|finished / done well|做好饭 (the meal is ready) · 准备好上课 (ready for class)|
|到 dào|to reach a place / time|回到家 (to get home) · 学到十一点 (to study until 11 pm)|
|到 dào|goal achieved|找到工作 (to find a job) · 买到飞机票 (to manage to buy the plane ticket)|
|见 jiàn|successful perception (after 看/听)|看见 (to see) · 听见 (to hear)|

> 💡 **看到 ≈ 看见** (to see) · **听到 ≈ 听见** (to hear)

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|我看见你的女朋友了。|Wǒ kànjiàn nǐ de nǚpéngyou le.|I saw your girlfriend.|
|我没听懂他说的话。|Wǒ méi tīng dǒng tā shuō de huà.|I didn't understand what he said.|
|两个星期以前 Maria 在找工作，现在她找到工作了。|Liǎng ge xīngqī yǐqián Maria zài zhǎo gōngzuò, xiànzài tā zhǎo dào gōngzuò le.|Two weeks ago Maria was looking for a job; now she's found one.|
|她在看书，她看到第几页了？— 她看到第550页了。|Tā zài kàn shū, tā kàn dào dì jǐ yè le? — Tā kàn dào dì 550 yè le.|She's reading; what page is she up to? — She's up to page 550.|
|你都做完（考试题）了没有？|Nǐ dōu zuò wán (kǎoshì tí) le méi yǒu?|Have you finished all (the exam questions)?|

---

### 2. The preposition 从 cóng — _"from, starting from"_

> 从 introduces the **starting point** of a period, distance, process, or sequence. Often with **到 dào** (until).
>
> **Structure: 从 + A (+ 到 + B) + ……**

|从|A|到|B|Rest|
|---|---|---|---|---|
|从|北京|到|上海|要坐几个小时的飞机？ (how many hours by plane?)|
|从|老人|到|孩子|都喜欢吃苹果。 (from the elderly to children, everyone likes apples)|
|从|下个星期一|||开始（上班）。 (he starts work next Monday)|

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|你从星期一到星期五都学中文吗？|Nǐ cóng xīngqī yī dào xīngqī wǔ dōu xué zhōngwén ma?|Do you study Chinese from Monday to Friday?|
|从这里坐公共汽车去机场要多长时间？|Cóng zhèli zuò gōnggòng qìchē qù jīchǎng yào duō cháng shíjiān?|From here, how long is it to the airport by bus?|
|他从什么时候开始上班？— 从下个星期一开始。|Tā cóng shénme shíhou kāishǐ shàngbān? — Cóng xià ge xīngqī yī kāishǐ.|When does he start work? — Starting next Monday.|

---

### 3. The ordinal 第 dì — _indicating order_

> **第 + Numeral + Measure word (+ Noun)** = the n-th.

|第|Num|Measure word|Noun|
|---|---|---|---|
|第|一|本|书 (the first book)|
|第|二|个|工作 (the second job)|
|第|一|次|跳舞 (the first time dancing)|

|Example|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|
|这是他的第一个工作。|Zhè shì tā de dì yī ge gōngzuò.|This is his first job.|
|我第一次跳舞是在七岁的时候。|Wǒ dì yī cì tiàowǔ shì zài qī suì de shíhou.|The first time I danced was when I was 7.|
|我们上到第几课了？|Wǒmen shàng dào dì jǐ kè le?|Which lesson are we up to?|

## 💬 Dialogues

---

### Dialogue 1 — 打电话 dǎ diànhuà (_On the phone_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|你好！请问张欢在吗？|Nǐ hǎo! Qǐngwèn Zhāng Huān zài ma?|Hello! Is Zhang Huan there, please?|
|B|你打错了，我们这儿没有叫张欢的。|Nǐ dǎ cuò le, wǒmen zhèr méiyǒu jiào Zhāng Huān de.|You've got the wrong number, there's no one called Zhang Huan here.|
|A|对不起。|Duìbuqǐ.|Sorry.|

> **Vocabulary:** 打错（电话）dǎ cuò (diànhuà) = to dial the wrong number

---

### Dialogue 2 — 在学校 zài xuéxiào (_At school_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|您从几岁开始学习跳舞？|Nín cóng jǐ suì kāishǐ xuéxí tiàowǔ?|At what age did you start learning dance?|
|B|我第一次跳舞是在七岁的时候。|Wǒ dì yī cì tiàowǔ shì zài qī suì de shíhou.|The first time I danced was when I was 7.|
|A|我女儿今年也七岁了。我希望她能跟您学跳舞，可以吗？|Wǒ nǚ'ér jīnnián yě qī suì le. Wǒ xīwàng tā néng gēn nín xué tiàowǔ, kěyǐ ma?|My daughter is also 7 this year. I hope she can learn dance with you, is that possible?|
|B|没问题，非常欢迎。|Méi wèntí, fēicháng huānyíng.|No problem, she's very welcome.|

> **Questions:** 老师从几岁开始学跳舞？老师想教她的女儿跳舞吗？

### Dialogue 3 — 在家里 zài jiā li (_At home_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|你知道吗？大卫找到工作了。|Nǐ zhīdào ma? Dàwèi zhǎodào gōngzuò le.|Did you know? David has found a job.|
|B|太好了！他从什么时候开始上班？|Tài hǎo le! Tā cóng shénme shíhou kāishǐ shàngbān?|Great! When does he start work?|

### Dialogue 4 — 在教室 zài jiàoshì (_In the classroom_)

||Chinese|Pinyin|Translation|
|---|---|---|---|
|A|昨天的考试怎么样？你都听懂了吗？|Zuótiān de kǎoshì zěnmeyàng? Nǐ dōu tīngdǒng le ma?|How was yesterday's exam? Did you understand everything?|
|B|听懂了。|Tīngdǒng le.|I understood.|

## 🔡 Phonetics

> **Intonation of yes-no questions (with 吗)**
>
> The **predicate is stressed** and the end of the sentence has a **rising intonation ↗**.
>
> Examples:
>
> - 你明天去**学校**吗？↗ (Are you going to school tomorrow?)
> - 他们都**知道**这件事吗？↗ (Do they all know about this?)
> - 你都**听懂**了吗？↗ (Did you understand everything?)

---

## ✍️ Characters

> **Radicals:**
>
> - **土** (_tí tǔ páng_): related to **earth, ground, buildings**. → e.g. 块 kuài (lump; monetary unit), 地 dì (earth, ground)
> - **灬** (_sì diǎn dǐ_, the "four dots"): related to **fire** and its use. → e.g. 热 rè (hot), 黑 hēi (black)

## 💡 Quick Recap

|Key point|Structure|Example|
|---|---|---|
|Resultative complement|V + 完/对/错/懂/好/到/见 + (O) + 了|我做完作业了|
|Negation of the complement|没 + V + complement (no 了!)|我没做完|
|Question with complement|……了没有？|你都做完了没有？|
|Finish doing|V + 完|吃完 / 喝完 / 做完|
|Do right / wrong|V + 对 / V + 错|写对了 / 打错电话了|
|Understand (spoken / written)|听懂 / 看懂|我没听懂|
|Goal achieved|V + 到|找到工作 / 买到机票|
|Perceive|看见 / 听见 (≈ 看到 / 听到)|我看见他了|
|From... to...|从 A 到 B|从星期一到星期五|
|Starting point|从 + time + 开始|从下个星期一开始|
|Ordinal|第 + Num + M.W.|第一次 / 第几课？|
|Hope|希望 + clause|希望他能喜欢|
|Work / finish work|上班 ↔ 下班|七点上班，五点下班|
|Welcome|欢迎 + 来/去 + place|欢迎来我家 / 欢迎光临！|
|Wrong number|打错（电话）了|你打错了|
